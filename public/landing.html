The framework behind the score

IDEA

Four dimensions of brand trust your customer's brain is checking at the moment of purchase — the heart of Trevor Bradford's book What Captures the Heart Goes in the Cart. The Trust Gap Score is a composite of all four.

I
Insight

A deep read of why your customers really buy — the emotional drivers under the demographics. Without it, the Assessor compares endlessly and never commits.

D
Distinctive

Standing for something, not just standing out. The Expresser buys who they want to be — features are irrelevant until identity is established.

E
Empathetic

Mirroring what the customer actually feels — safety, belonging, validation. The Protector must feel understood before it will engage with any evidence.

A
Authentic

Values and promises kept in actions, not words. The Connector decides whether your brand deserves a place in their life before considering the product.

Straight answers

Questions, answered honestly

Is this a brand strategy tool? +
No. It's a conversion problem solver. It produces brand-strategy outputs — positioning, a Brand Canvas — but only as a consequence of the diagnostic, never as the destination. The front-end promise is finding the one thing stopping the sale.
What do I actually get back? +
Available
A Trust Gap Score out of 100 across four pillars, your primary Decision Trigger, the verbatim review evidence behind it, and a design brief (image + copy + CTA) you can hand off.
How is this different from asking ChatGPT? +
ChatGPT reads what customers say. This reads what their language reveals about the emotional state they're in when they buy — through a framework (Four Buyer States, Trust Gap, Decision Trigger) that no general AI has, regardless of prompt.
What if my product has very few reviews? +
Roadmap
With under ~50 reviews the classification is less certain. A competitor-corpus supplement — reading the category's review language alongside yours — is on the roadmap to make new brands first-class.
Will it re-measure after I ship the fix? +
Roadmap
The Brand Defence loop — re-measure and defend — is what we're building toward. Today the coach takes you through Diagnose → Analyse → Fix.
Do I need an account to try it? +
Available
No. The free web diagnostic and the Claude connector both start without one. You only sign in when you want to save and revisit your work.
