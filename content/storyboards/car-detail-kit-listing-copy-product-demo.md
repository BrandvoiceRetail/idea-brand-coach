## Strategy

His felt pain, in his own words: *"My bullets say 'premium microfiber' and 'streak-free finish.' So does every other kit on this page. I don't even know what my customers actually care about."* He wrote generic, safe copy because he never systematically looked at what buyers were praising.

The funnel position is `amazon_listing_copy`: the moment after the click where generic bullets fail to separate his kit from five near-identical competitors, because nothing in the copy reflects what real buyers say made the difference.

The hook runs on **momentum**: this isn't a copywriting project or a redesign cycle — it's a same-session fix once the actual review evidence is on the table.

## Hook variants

1. **Momentum:** "I didn't write a new bullet. I found the one my customers already wrote for me — in about ten minutes."
2. **Permission:** "You don't need a copywriter for this. You need your own reviews and ten minutes."
3. **Recognition:** "My bullets said 'premium.' My customers said something a lot more specific."

## Production notes (Higgsfield)

- **Mode:** Per-scene precision — this is an entirely screen-capture-led product demo (real UI states, real parsing progress, real before/after copy) requiring exact control scene by scene.
- **Reference kit:** Screen-UI mock of the coach's chat, the `ingest_evidence` parsing state, and the flagged-mismatch results panel; real product photo of the detailing kit for any listing-context insert; founder character sheet only if an on-camera cutaway is added in edit.
- **Negative prompt / no-text rule:** No text, labels, or captions baked into generated UI-mock frames — every caption and quoted phrase is a post-production overlay per the TEXT ON SCREEN field; do not fabricate additional review quotes or star counts beyond what's specified.
- **Diegetic audio:** Soft keyboard and trackpad sounds timed to the paste-and-run action, subtle UI progress chime when parsing completes, no music bed until the end-card sting.

## CTA & measurement

**End card copy:** "Feed it your reviews — free." **Destination:** ideabrandcoach.com/diagnostic. **Metric that proves the ad worked:** click-through rate from the ad to /diagnostic — the momentum hook promises a same-session fix, so a lift in immediate click-through (versus a delayed save-for-later) is the signal that promise landed. Downstream, sessions-to-orders on the listing is what proves the rewritten bullets, not just the demo, moved the needle.
