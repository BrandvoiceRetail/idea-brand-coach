## Strategy

The felt pain, in her words: "I rewrote the intro twice. Same flat number both times. I was starting to think the post was just cursed." Eight weeks of a well-ranked post underperforming its `seo_content` position because she kept touching the wrong variable — the body copy — instead of the one line that actually earns the click: the title. `design_test` solves the connection problem by isolating that untouched variable and structuring a real hypothesis (benefit-led vs. keyword-led title) with a defined before/after comparison window, replacing the guess-and-republish cycle with one measurable afternoon.

The hook plays **momentum** — problem first (two months of rewrites, zero movement), then the specific relief of a test that took an afternoon instead of another sprint.

## Hook variants

1. **momentum** — "Two rewrites. Two months. Zero movement. Then we tested one line."
2. **recognition** — "If you're ranking fine and getting clicked rarely, you've probably rewritten the wrong thing."
3. **permission** — "The post wasn't broken. I just hadn't tested the one part I never touch — the title."

## Production notes (Higgsfield)

- **Mode:** storyboard-image for scenes 1-3 (calm screen/desk problem-first setup fits a single multi-panel plate); per-scene precision for scenes 4-5 where the design_test comparison panel and results card need to read cleanly as distinct UI states.
- **Reference kit:** persona character sheet (same founder, same desk setting, consistent across scenes 1, 2, 3, 5); screen-UI mock of the `design_test` two-variant title comparison and its CTR results card, built from the real in-app layout, not invented UI chrome.
- **Negative prompt / no-text-in-frame:** exclude any burned-in captions, chart labels, or title-variant copy rendered directly into the generated visuals beyond what's explicitly specified as in-scene UI text — all TEXT ON SCREEN overlay lines are composited in post.
- **Diegetic audio:** quiet home-office ambience under scenes 1-2; a soft "two failed attempts" tone under scene 2's rewrite montage; near-silent under 3-4 so VO and coach-UI carry it; a light resolving chime under the results reveal in scene 5.
