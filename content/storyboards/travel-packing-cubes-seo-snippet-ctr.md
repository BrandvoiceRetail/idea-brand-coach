## Strategy

Her felt pain: *"I'm ranked page one, position one-point-two. Nobody's clicking through."* Ranking is supposed to be the hard part — she did that — and the traffic still isn't showing up, which feels like there's nothing left to fix.

The funnel position is `seo_content`: the search-results snippet itself is a click decision, separate from the ranking algorithm. A page-one position with a generic meta title and no proof point in the preview text loses to weaker-ranked competitors with a sharper snippet.

The hook runs on **fear_of_loss** — a page-one ranking nobody clicks isn't a near-win, it's a wasted position she already earned and is bleeding.

## Hook variants

1. **Fear_of_loss:** "I'm ranked page one, position one-point-two. Nobody's clicking through."
2. **Recognition:** "Ranking well and getting clicked are not the same win."
3. **Momentum:** "Fixing this wasn't a rewrite. It was one line of snippet copy."
