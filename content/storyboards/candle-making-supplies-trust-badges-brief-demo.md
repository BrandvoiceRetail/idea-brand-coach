## Strategy

"I can feel something's off right where people decide to buy, but I can't name
it" is the felt pain — a real hunch with no diagnosis behind it, so nothing ever
gets fixed. The diagnosis and the fix both land at **trust_badges_social_proof**:
the coach names the weak signal in one pass, then `generate_brief` compresses
that finding into a one-page brief the founder's designer can execute this week.
The hook plays **momentum** — a vague hunch becomes a shippable brief in one
sitting, not another week of second-guessing the buy box.

## Hook variants

- **momentum** — "I had a hunch something was off near my buy button. One session later, I had a brief."
- **recognition** — "The coach named the exact thing I could feel but never could point at."
- **permission** — "I didn't need a full redesign. I needed one page, and this got me there."

## Production notes (Higgsfield)

- **Mode:** storyboard-image (screen-recording product demo — single multi-panel storyboard image driving one video job for consistent pacing across the scan-to-brief arc).
- **Reference kit:** persona character sheet for the founder (brief on-camera appearance, scenes 1 and 4-5); screen-UI mock of the coach's trust_badges_social_proof finding and the `generate_brief` one-page output (placeholder spec content, not real Amazon buy-box screenshots).
- **Negative-prompt / no-text-in-frame:** no baked-in on-screen text inside generated video frames — TEXT ON SCREEN is a post overlay only; no real Amazon Seller Central UI chrome; no fabricated certification or badge claims rendered as real.
- **Diegetic audio:** quiet home-studio room tone, soft click/keyboard foley under the screen-recording beats, no music bed until the CTA card.

## CTA & measurement

End card: "Free diagnostic. 6 questions. No account." Destination:
**ideabrandcoach.com/diagnostic**. The metric that proves this ad worked:
**click-through rate on the ad's own link** to the diagnostic landing page —
naming a hunch shoppers actually recognize should out-click a generic
"boost your conversion rate" pitch. Downstream, once the brief ships, the
metric that proves the fix itself worked is **sessions-to-orders on
trust_badges_social_proof** — the real cvr lift, not just this ad's click
performance.
