## Strategy

The felt pain, in her words: "Every quarter I'd rewrite the same post, hope for better clicks, watch the number stay flat, and tell myself I was 'staying on top of it.'" The funnel position is `seo_content` — a recurring content-refresh habit that felt like progress but never touched the actual variable that moves click-through. `design_test` solves the connection problem by replacing "let's try a new title" — a vague intention — with an actual hypothesis, a defined metric, and a real comparison window, so she finally knows whether a change worked instead of just feeling busy.

The hook plays **permission** — stopping the quarterly rewrite treadmill isn't giving up on the post, it's the first time she's actually measured anything.

## Hook variants

1. **permission** — "Stopping the rewrite treadmill wasn't giving up. It was the first time I actually measured anything."
2. **recognition** — "If your 'quarterly refresh' has never once moved your click-through rate, you know this cycle."
3. **momentum** — "Same post, three rewrites, zero proof any of them worked. Until I tested one."

## Production notes (Higgsfield)

- **Mode:** storyboard-image (6 scenes, 30s founder-story format; consistent home-studio setting and calm pacing suit one multi-panel storyboard image driving a single video job).
- **Reference kit:** persona character sheet for the founder (same face, same home-studio setting across scenes 1, 2, 3, 5); product reference sheet from the real cork-yoga-mat photo for background dressing; screen-UI mock of the `design_test` hypothesis/metric setup and its results card, built from the real in-app layout.
