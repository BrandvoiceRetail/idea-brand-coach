## Strategy

"I doubled my ad spend and my clicks didn't move — only my cost-per-click did"
is the felt pain, the textbook rising-cost, flat-CTR trap. The diagnosis sits at
**paid_social_creative**: the "ad" was never a new asset, it was her main image
with a discount sticker slapped on, so more spend just bought more impressions of
the same non-clicking creative. `generate_main_image_title_plan` fixes the
connection problem by rebuilding the positioning statement — brand, the real
keyword, the specific difference — before it goes back into the ad, so the fix is
in the words, not a reshoot. The hook plays **permission** — she didn't need a
video shoot, she needed the right eleven words.

## Hook variants

- **permission** — "I thought I needed a video shoot. I needed eleven different words."
- **fear_of_loss** — "I doubled my ad spend and all that changed was how much each click cost me."
- **recognition** — "My 'ad' was just my main image with a discount sticker slapped on it."

## Production notes (Higgsfield)

- **Mode:** per-scene precision — the seven beats (dashboard, sticker-ad, tool screen, output, drag-in, before/after split, CTA) need distinct staging rather than one multi-panel storyboard image.
- **Reference kit:** persona character sheet for the founder (consistent across live-action beats); screen-UI mocks for the generic ad-dashboard, the coach tool interface, and the before/after ad-creative comparison card; product reference sheet from the real resistance-bands packshot for the "sticker ad" beat.
- **Negative-prompt / no-text-in-frame:** no real Meta/Instagram trademarked UI chrome rendered; no baked-in on-screen text inside generated frames — every TEXT ON SCREEN line is a post overlay; dashboards and charts shown as illustrative mockups, never presented as verified real analytics.
- **Diegetic audio:** light room tone throughout, mouse clicks and keyboard taps during the screen-share beats, no score or music sting until the final CTA card.

## CTA & measurement

End card: "Run the free diagnostic before your next reshoot." Destination:
**ideabrandcoach.com/diagnostic**. The one metric that proves this ad worked:
**click-through rate on this ad's own link-click campaign** — proof that naming
the real positioning problem, not production value, is what drives the click.
