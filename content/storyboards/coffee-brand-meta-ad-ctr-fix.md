## Strategy

"Ninety thousand people saw my ad. Thirty-eight clicked" is the felt pain — reach
did its job, but the creative didn't. The diagnosis lands at
**paid_social_creative**, because her ad isn't a separate asset with its own
problem — it's her Amazon packshot, the same broken positioning statement, just
paid to reach more people. `generate_main_image_title_plan` fixes the connection
problem once, at the source, so the same fixed statement carries across both
placements instead of needing two separate rebuilds. The hook plays
**fear_of_loss** — every dollar of spend is currently buying impressions for an
image that was already failing to earn a click.

## Hook variants

- **fear_of_loss** — "I just paid to show ninety thousand people an image that was already failing to get clicked."
- **recognition** — "My Meta ad and my Amazon listing are the same photo — no wonder they both flop the same way."
- **permission** — "I didn't need a new photoshoot for my ad. I needed the same eleven words fixed, once."

## Production notes (Higgsfield)

- **Mode:** storyboard-image default — a single multi-panel storyboard image (dashboard, packshot-ad, tool screen, split-compare, publish) rendered into one video job, since this is screen-recording-style product demo, not live performance.
- **Reference kit:** screen-UI mock of a generic ad-dashboard (no real Meta chrome), screen-UI mock of the coach's tool output card, persona character sheet for the founder's hands/desk continuity across the cutaway shots.
- **Negative-prompt / no-text-in-frame:** no real Meta/Facebook or Amazon trademarked UI rendered; no baked-in on-screen text inside generated frames — all copy is a post-production overlay; no fabricated or illegible numbers baked into the mock dashboard beyond the two called out in VO; the 90,000/38 figures are framed as this composite founder's illustrative scenario, never presented as a verified real result.
- **Diegetic audio:** soft mouse clicks and keyboard taps during the screen-share beats, quiet room tone; no music bed until the CTA card.

## CTA & measurement

End card: "Stop paying to show a losing image. Free diagnostic, 6 questions."
Destination: **ideabrandcoach.com/diagnostic**. The one metric that proves this ad
worked: **click-through rate on this ad's own link** to the diagnostic — the ad's
job is to convert the exact frustration it dramatizes into a click.
