## Strategy

Mara's felt pain, in her own words: "I unbox my own product and there's nothing in the box worth keeping. Just the mat, and a card with a QR code on it." At `packaging_unboxing`, the insert card is the only thing besides the product itself that a customer sees up close — and hers is asking for a follow, a review, a link click, with zero reason attached.

`generate_signature` gives that card a line that's actually the brand's — something distinctive enough to read twice, not just scan and toss. The hook runs on **momentum**: this isn't a packaging redesign, it's a same-day swap of the one blank card already in every box.

## Hook variants

1. **Momentum:** "My insert card has exactly one thing on it. A QR code. That's a five-minute fix, not a redesign."
2. **Recognition:** "You've unboxed something with a card just like this — blank, forgettable, straight into the recycling."
3. **Fear_of_loss:** "Every unboxing without a line worth remembering is a customer who forgets which brand this was."

## Production notes (Higgsfield)

- **Mode:** Storyboard-image default — one multi-panel storyboard covers the unboxing, the card close-up, and the tool-to-redesign reveal in a single video job; the product-demo format is simple enough not to need per-scene precision.
- **Reference kit:** Persona character sheet for Mara (consistent face, home-office wardrobe, desk setup); product reference sheet for the standing desk mat and the blank insert card from real product photos; a flat screen-UI mock of the coach chat and the redesigned insert-card mock for scenes 3-4.
- **Negative prompt / no-text-in-frame:** Do not render legible card copy, QR-code detail, or UI labels inside generated frames — supply the blank card and the redesigned card as flat reference images and add every TEXT ON SCREEN line as a post overlay. On-screen disclosure: "Actor portrayal. Composite scenario for illustration." — Mara is a fictional composite founder, not a verified buyer.
- **Diegetic audio:** Quiet home-office room tone, cardboard-and-tissue rustle during the unboxing beat, soft trackpad clicks under scene 3, no music bed until the end card.

## CTA & measurement

End card copy: "Free Trust Gap diagnostic. See what your packaging's missing." Destination: `ideabrandcoach.com/diagnostic`. **Metrics that prove it worked:** hook rate and diagnostic-start rate among ad-attributed clicks prove the momentum angle landed; the metric that proves the connection problem itself is fixed is repeat-purchase rate among customers who received the new insert card, plus how often that card shows up photographed and tagged instead of tossed.
