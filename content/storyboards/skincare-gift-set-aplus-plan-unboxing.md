## Strategy

Her felt pain: *"I was one click from publishing this. It's just my bullet points in a bigger font."* She built her A+ content off a template she found online, proud it looked polished, and never noticed it said nothing her listing bullets hadn't already said.

The funnel position is `amazon_brand_story`: the module a shopper reaches after she's already read the bullets, deciding whether there's a real brand behind the gift set worth trusting for someone else's skin. Restating the same claim five times in nicer fonts gives that shopper zero new reason to buy. `generate_aplus_content_plan` rebuilds the module as 5 addressable beats instead of one repeated idea.

Hook trigger: recognition — the "oh, it really is just the bullets again" moment, caught before publish instead of after.

## Hook variants

1. **Recognition** — "I almost published this. It's just my bullet points in a bigger font."
2. **Permission** — "You don't need a photographer to fix A+ content. You need five beats, not five banners."
3. **Fear of Loss** — "Every shopper who scrolls past without learning something new is a sale I already lost."

## Production notes (Higgsfield)

- **Mode:** marketing-studio UGC preset (unboxing format holds hand, box, and product continuity across a single continuous take).
- **Reference kit:** persona character sheet for the founder; product sheet built from the real gift-set photo; two distinct printed-mockup props (old five-banner sheet vs. new five-beat sheet) so the before/after reads clearly on camera; screen-UI mock of the coach's plan-builder for scene 3.
- **Negative prompt / no-text rule:** no baked-in captions or UI text rendered inside generated video frames — every TEXT ON SCREEN line is a post overlay. Do not render fabricated certification marks or platform badges on the box or printouts.
- **Diegetic audio:** box-cutter scrape and paper-rustle under narration, no music bed until the end-card sting.

## CTA & measurement

**End card copy:** "Don't publish another banner. Publish a story. Free diagnostic." **Destination:** ideabrandcoach.com/diagnostic. **Metric that proves the ad worked:** click-through rate from this ad to `/diagnostic` — proof the recognition hook ("it's just my bullets again") gets founders to check their own brand story module before they publish, not after. The metric that proves the *fix* worked, once she publishes the new beats: sessions-to-orders on her listing — the actual cvr movement, not just the click.
