## Strategy

Her felt pain, in her own words: *"I changed my whole positioning three months ago. My welcome email didn't get the memo."* Her brand's signature line moved on — sharper, more specific — but `welcome_series` step one never heard about it and still opens on the old generic pitch.

The funnel position is `welcome_series`: the sequence every new subscriber runs through first, meaning every new person for three months got sold a version of the brand that no longer exists anywhere else. `refine_creative_plan` propagates the new positioning across every step in the sequence at once, without a manual rewrite or a rebuild.

Hook trigger: momentum — the fix isn't writing new emails, it's catching the ones still running on the old story.

## Hook variants

1. **Momentum** — "I changed my whole positioning three months ago. My welcome email didn't get the memo."
2. **Recognition** — "New signature line everywhere — except the one email every new customer actually reads."
3. **Fear_of_loss** — "Every new subscriber this month got sold my old brand. I didn't even notice."
