## Strategy

The felt pain, in the founder's own words: "In April this ad was working. By June, same creative, same targeting, and it just stopped." A composite founder we'll call Denise sells raised garden bed kits. Her ad's CTR tracked the season — strong in spring, dead by early summer — and she assumed it was just seasonal ad fatigue.

The connection problem sits at `paid_social_creative`: the ad kept leading with bed dimensions and material specs, a pitch that works when someone's actively comparison-shopping in March but goes flat once the "should I even start a garden this year" window closes. The coach runs `identify_decision_trigger` and finds the real lever isn't dimensions at all — it's **identity**. Buyers aren't shopping for a bed; they're shopping for the version of themselves who finally grows their own food this year.

The hook swaps the specs-first opener for a direct identity call, delivered in review-style monologue by the founder herself.

## Hook variants

1. **identity:** "You're not shopping for a garden bed. You're shopping for the year you actually grow something."
2. **momentum:** "Spring's almost gone. The window to start this year is closing, not closed."
3. **recognition:** "You've bookmarked this bed three times. That's not indecision, that's a yes you haven't said yet."

## Production notes (Higgsfield)

- **Mode:** marketing-studio preset (UGC review) — this is a straight-to-camera testimonial-style format; the preset handles the natural handheld framing and pacing better than a manual storyboard build.
- **Reference kit:** founder character sheet for Denise (consistent face, outdoor wardrobe, garden setting across all live-action scenes); a product reference sheet from the real raised-bed-kit photos for the old-ad and new-concept inserts; a screen-UI mock of the coach's trigger-result card.
- **Negative prompt / no-text-in-frame:** no baked-in captions or graph labels rendered into the video frames — the CTR graph numbers and all TEXT ON SCREEN elements are composited as a separate overlay layer.
- **Diegetic audio:** natural outdoor ambience (birds, light wind) under all live-action scenes since this is a straight UGC monologue; a soft UI ping when the trigger result renders in scene 3; brand sting under the scene 5 CTA card only.

## CTA & measurement

- **End card copy:** "Find the trigger your ad's been missing." with the ideabrandcoach.com wordmark.
- **Destination:** ideabrandcoach.com/diagnostic
- **The one metric that proves the ad worked:** click-through rate on the ad itself, specifically whether it holds steady past the seasonal drop-off point the founder describes — a flat-or-rising CTR into summer is the tell the identity-led hook is doing its job.
