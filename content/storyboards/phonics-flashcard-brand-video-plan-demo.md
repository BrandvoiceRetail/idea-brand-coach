## Strategy

Her own words: "I get one shoot. If the ad doesn't work, there's no budget to reshoot it." That's the pressure — no room to learn by trial and error on camera.

The funnel position is `paid_social_creative`: before a single frame is filmed, the ad's structure needs to already be right. `generate_video_storyboard` plans the scene-by-scene ad — hook, three trigger-angled openings to test, a defined CTA beat — so the one shoot she can afford is built around a tested plan instead of a guess.

Hook trigger: permission. She's allowed to plan before she spends, instead of chasing perfection after.

## Hook variants

1. **Permission** — "You don't need three shoots to find the right ad. You need one shoot built around the right plan."
2. **Momentum** — "We mapped the whole ad before we booked a single hour of filming."
3. **Recognition** — "I had one shot at this shoot, and I almost wasted it on the wrong opening."
