## Strategy

The felt pain, in her words: *"I wrote my About page two years ago and never touched it again."* `brand_store_about` is a single generic paragraph — nothing to repeat, nothing to remember, and every packing-cube brand says the same "travel smarter" line.

`generate_storefront_messaging_plan` rebuilds the whole page as one system — hero, brand story, a signature-derived tagline, and job-mapped category tiles — built around who's actually buying: the traveler who over-packs and already knows it.

The hook runs on **identity** — the page finally speaks to a specific traveler instead of "everyone."

## Hook variants

1. **Identity:** "My About page is one paragraph. It doesn't even know my best customers over-pack."
2. **Belonging:** "Every packing-cube brand says 'travel smarter.' Mine did too. It never once said 'you've found your people.'"
3. **Recognition:** "Two years, one paragraph, and it never once told my customer: I get it."
