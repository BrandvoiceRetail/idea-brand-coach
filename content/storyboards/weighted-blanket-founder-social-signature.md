## Strategy

The felt pain, in her words: *"'We started this because we couldn't sleep.'"* — the opening line of her `founder_social` reel, and the opening line of nearly every other weighted-blanket brand's reel too.

`generate_signature` produces the one distinctive line that's actually hers — built from the specific fear her customers describe, not the generic founding story every brand in the category reaches for first.

The hook runs on **fear_of_loss** — the exact 3 a.m., wide-awake feeling the blanket fixes, said back to the viewer instead of buried under an origin story.

## Hook variants

1. **Fear_of_loss:** "Delete your founder video's first line. It's costing you the scroll."
2. **Identity:** "My founder video sounded like every other 'our story' reel. So I deleted the story."
3. **Recognition:** "Every weighted-blanket founder opens with 'we started this because we couldn't sleep.' So did mine. Not anymore."

## Production notes (Higgsfield)

- **Mode:** Per-scene precision — the comparison beats (her video vs. generic competitor grid vs. new line) need distinct panel control rather than one continuous storyboard image.
- **Reference kit:** Founder character sheet for scene-1 continuity; screen-UI mocks of the video-editor timeline, a generic non-trademarked competitor-reel grid, and the `generate_signature` output panel; real product photo of the weighted blanket.
- **Negative prompt / no-text-in-frame:** No legible platform chrome, logos, or real competitor branding baked into generated frames — render generic mock UI only; add every TEXT ON SCREEN line as a post overlay. On-screen disclosure required: "Actor portrayal. Composite scenario for illustration."
- **Diegetic audio:** Quiet home room tone throughout; laptop trackpad/typing SFX under scenes 1-4; no music bed until the end card.

## CTA & measurement

**End card copy:** "Find your one line. Free diagnostic." **Destination:** ideabrandcoach.com/diagnostic. **Ad-level metric:** click-through rate from the ad to /diagnostic — the fear_of_loss hook targets founders whose social content sounds generic, so a lift proves the ad convinced them their own opening line is costing them the scroll. **Connection-level metric:** branded search lift after the new opening line ships — proof the fear language landed hard enough that viewers go looking for the brand instead of scrolling past it.
