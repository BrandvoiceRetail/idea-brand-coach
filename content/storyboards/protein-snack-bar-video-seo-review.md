## Strategy

Her own words: "My video gets watched. My blog post still doesn't get clicked." Two different problems hiding behind one healthy-looking analytics dashboard.

The funnel position is `seo_content`: the video and the page's search snippet were built separately and don't reinforce each other — different promises, same page. `generate_video_storyboard`'s storyboard-image mode aligns the video's opening beat with the exact phrase the page ranks for, so the search snippet and the video promise the same specific payoff.

Hook trigger: momentum. One clear promise, repeated at both entry points, instead of two half-hooks.

## Hook variants

1. **Momentum** — "My video and my search snippet were making two different promises. I only needed one."
2. **Recognition** — "Good watch time. Flat click-through. Turns out those are two different problems."
3. **Permission** — "You don't need a new video. You need the video and the snippet to agree."
