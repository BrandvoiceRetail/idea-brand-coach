## Strategy

Her felt pain, in her own words: *"I have twenty photos of my own leggings. Not one of them shows what they feel like to move in."* Every gallery slide proves the product exists — fabric, waistband, stitching — and none of them prove the feeling a shopper is actually buying.

The funnel position is `amazon_listing_copy`: specifically the gallery order, the point where a shopper who already clicked in is deciding whether to trust that this garment will do something for *her* body, under real movement, not just look correct in a flat-lay. `generate_listing_image_brief` builds the full image-SET brief — main plus every gallery slot — and names the one she's missing: the photoreal lifestyle "felt moment" slot.

The hook runs on **identity** — she can't see herself, a real body moving under pressure, anywhere in her own gallery.

## Hook variants

1. **Identity:** "My gallery proves these leggings exist. It's never once proven what they feel like on a real body under pressure."
2. **Recognition:** "Six gallery slides in and I still couldn't tell you how these leggings feel to run in."
3. **Fear_of_loss:** "Every day I leave my gallery like this, I'm handing that sale to someone else."

## Production notes (Higgsfield)

- **Mode:** Marketing-studio preset (UGC try-on) — mirror-selfie-to-camera plus a phone/laptop screen cutaway is exactly the preset's target shape.
- **Reference kit:** Persona character sheet for the founder-actor (consistent face/wardrobe across scenes); product reference sheet built from the real activewear set's product photos; screen-UI mock of the coach chat and the gallery-brief slot list for scenes 3-4.
- **Negative prompt / no-text-in-frame:** No baked-in captions or UI labels inside generated frames — every TEXT ON SCREEN line is added as a post overlay. On-screen disclosure required: "Actor portrayal. Composite scenario for illustration." — a fictional founder persona, never implied as a verified buyer or real listing data.
- **Diegetic audio:** Quiet bedroom/closet room tone, soft fabric-rustle as she gestures to the outfit, laptop trackpad clicks under scenes 3-4, no music bed until the end card.
