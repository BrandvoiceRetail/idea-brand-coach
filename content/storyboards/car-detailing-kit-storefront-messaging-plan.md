## Strategy

The felt pain, in the founder's words: *"New customers every week. Repeat buyers? Almost none. My storefront sells the kit — it never gives anyone a reason to come back."* One-and-done buyers keep vanishing after the first order, and he's been blaming the product instead of the page nobody remembers.

The funnel position is `brand_store_about`: the Brand Store's home turf, meant to turn a first-time buyer into someone who thinks of this brand specifically the next time their kit runs low. His storefront restates the product ("premium detailing kit") instead of building a reason to return. `generate_storefront_messaging_plan` rebuilds the hero, a tagline system, and job-mapped category tiles around what happens *after* the first buy.

The hook runs on **momentum** — the fix isn't a rebrand or a redesign project, it's one plan built in a single coach session.
