## Strategy

The felt pain, in the founder's words: *"Handpoured with love. Clean-burning soy wax. Swap my brand name for the one next to me and nobody would notice."* Her Brand Store About page is technically fine and completely interchangeable — the copy could belong to any candle brand on the page.

The funnel position is `brand_store_about`: the module meant to make a browsing shopper remember this specific brand instead of the next candle listing they'll scroll to in ten seconds. `generate_signature` produces the one distinctive line that's actually hers — not a better version of the generic template, a line nobody else could have written.

The hook runs on **identity** — the line has to sound like it could only come from this founder, not from a candle-brand copy template.

## Hook variants

1. **Identity:** "I could swap my About page with any other candle brand's and nobody would notice."
2. **Recognition:** "Scent notes. 'Handpoured with care.' I've read this exact About page on ten other listings."
3. **Momentum:** "One line replaced my whole headline. Same session."

## Production notes (Higgsfield)

- **Mode:** Per-scene precision — this is a screen-capture-led demo (real UI states, a live headline swap) that needs exact control scene by scene, not one multi-panel storyboard image.
- **Reference kit:** Founder character sheet only if an on-camera cutaway is added in edit; screen-UI mock of the coach's chat and the `generate_signature` drafting panel; screen-UI mock of the Brand Store About page before and after; real product photo of the soy candle line.
- **Negative prompt / no-text rule:** No text, labels, or captions baked into generated UI-mock frames — every caption and the signature line itself (including the scene 4 headline) is a post-production overlay per the TEXT ON SCREEN field, never rendered into the image directly.
- **Diegetic audio:** Soft trackpad clicks and a subtle UI-generation chime timed to the drafting beat, quiet home-office room tone, no music bed until the end-card sting.
- **Edit-before-regenerate:** if the About-page mock needs a layout fix after the headline swap, edit that generation directly rather than re-rendering the before/after pair from scratch.
