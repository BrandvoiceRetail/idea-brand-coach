## Strategy

Her felt pain, in her own words: *"My Instagram sounds like me. My welcome email sounds like a shipping label."* Her social posts are personal — why she started carving toys, who she's making them for — but the first email a new customer ever gets from her, `welcome_series` step one, reads like a generic order receipt.

The funnel position is `welcome_series`: the sequence meant to turn a first purchase into a relationship, opening at the exact moment a new customer is most curious about who they just bought from. `create_email_sequence` builds the 5-step sequence so step one opens in her real voice, carrying the same belonging her social content already earns instead of resetting to zero.

Hook trigger: belonging — the customer already felt close to her on social; the welcome email is where that closeness gets thrown away.

## Hook variants

1. **Belonging** — "My Instagram sounds like me. My welcome email sounds like a shipping label."
2. **Recognition** — "Order confirmed. Thanks for your purchase. That's not a hello, that's a receipt."
3. **Permission** — "You're allowed to let the first email sound like you, not like legal copy."

## Production notes (Higgsfield)

- **Mode:** Storyboard-image default — one continuous workshop setting across a calm founder narrative; a single multi-panel storyboard image drives one video job.
- **Reference kit:** Founder character sheet for continuity across all six shots; product reference sheet built from real wooden-toy photos for the workbench dressing; screen-UI mocks of the Instagram caption, the flat welcome email, and the coach's 5-step sequence builder output.
- **Negative prompt / no-text-in-frame:** No baked-in captions or UI copy inside generated frames — every TEXT ON SCREEN line is composited in post; render both email mocks as clean generic layouts, not a trademarked ESP screenshot.
- **Diegetic audio:** Workshop room tone throughout — light sanding and wood-tool sounds under scene 1; a phone-notification tap under scene 2; quiet laptop-key sounds under scenes 4-5; no music bed until the end card.
