## Strategy

Her felt pain, in her own words: *"Take twelve. 'We started this in our kitchen.' Every founder video I make opens the same way, and none of them convert."* She keeps reshooting the same origin story, assuming the problem is delivery, not the story itself.

The funnel position is `founder_social`: the video content meant to build the connection a listing page can't — the moment a scroller decides whether this founder, this brand, is theirs. `identify_decision_trigger` names the ONE psychological lever her specialty cold-brew buyers actually turn on: not the origin story, but **belonging** — wanting to feel like part of a group of people who already know the difference between good coffee and great coffee.

The hook runs on **belonging** — the fix isn't a better story, it's speaking to the group her buyer already wants to be recognized as part of.

## Hook variants

1. **Belonging:** "Everyone at my local market already has strong opinions about coffee. My video wasn't talking to them."
2. **Recognition:** "My founder video says 'we started this in our kitchen.' So does every other coffee brand's."
3. **Momentum:** "One line. That's all it took to fix eighteen months of the same video."

## Production notes (Higgsfield)

- **Mode:** storyboard-image default — a single continuous kitchen setting across a linear personal narrative; one multi-panel storyboard image drives one video job.
- **Reference kit:** Founder character sheet for continuity across all six shots; screen-UI mocks of the coach's chat, the `identify_decision_trigger` trigger-narrowing panel, and the past-videos grid; real product photo of the cold-brew coffee bag.
- **Negative prompt / no-text rule:** No baked-in on-screen text or captions in generated frames; render the video-thumbnail grid as generic mocks, never trademarked platform chrome or logos. On-screen disclosure required: "Actor portrayal. Composite scenario for illustration."
- **Diegetic audio:** Quiet kitchen ambience and a coffee-bag rustle under scene 1, laptop-typing SFX under scenes 2-4, a slight energy lift in room tone for the re-record in scene 5, no music bed until the end card.
- **Edit-before-regenerate:** if the trigger-narrowing panel in scene 4 needs a wording pass, edit that generation directly rather than re-rendering the kitchen sequence, so the founder's face and setting stay consistent across all six shots.
