## Strategy

The felt pain: "Add-to-carts look fine. They just don't turn into checkouts." A composite founder we'll call Noor sells an ergonomic seat cushion for home-office chairs. Her add-to-cart rate looks healthy on the dashboard — shoppers want the product. Something between "added" and "bought" is quietly killing the sale.

The connection problem sits at `trust_badges_social_proof`: the buy-box moment right before checkout, where a shopper needs one more reason to feel safe clicking "buy." The coach runs `run_trust_gap` on that section and finds **Authentic** scoring lowest — no return-policy or material-safety cue anywhere near the button, the missing signal that would give a hesitant shopper permission to buy, not a missing discount.

The hook plays **permission**: shoppers need something that tells them it's safe to click buy, and right now nothing does.

## Hook variants

1. **permission:** "Nobody needs a discount to buy this. They need something that says it's safe to click buy."
2. **recognition:** "Add-to-carts look fine. Checkouts don't follow. I didn't know where to even look."
3. **fear_of_loss:** "Every cart that doesn't check out is a sale I already had — and lost anyway."

## Production notes (Higgsfield)

- **Mode:** Per-scene precision — the dashboard-to-buy-box-to-scorecard progression needs distinct, controllable UI states.
- **Reference kit:** Founder character sheet for Noor (home-office desk setting — on camera in scene 1 only; scene 5 is a pure wordmark end card with no founder cutaway); real seat-cushion product photography for desk cutaways; screen-UI mock of the coach's chat and Trust Gap scorecard flagging `trust_badges_social_proof`; a buy-box before/after mock for scene 4.
- **Negative prompt / no-text-in-frame:** No baked-in text or real Amazon trademarked UI chrome in generated frames — captions are post overlays only. Do not render a fabricated certification logo or invented policy claim; the added lines are illustrative of the fix, not an asserted real policy.
- **Diegetic audio:** Quiet home-office ambience, soft typing and mouse-click sounds under the screen-record beats, brand sting on the end card.

## CTA & measurement

**End card copy:** "Find your missing signal — free." **Destination:** ideabrandcoach.com/diagnostic. **Metric that proves the ad worked:** click-through rate from the ad to /diagnostic — a trust-gap ad has to earn the click before it can prove anything about the checkout gap. **Metric that proves the underlying fix worked:** sessions-to-orders (the add-to-cart-to-checkout step specifically) once the return-policy and safety-cue lines are live near the buy button.
