## Strategy

Her own words: *"I filmed myself unboxing my own product to check it. Even I set the insert card down without reading it."* That's the felt pain — forty cents of print, and not even the founder gives it a second look.

The funnel position is `packaging_unboxing`: the single moment attention is highest, competing directly with the trash. Right now the card is a receipt with a QR code — nothing that says who the carrier is actually for. Her wider funnel — listing images, storefront, welcome email — is already built and live; she doesn't want to touch any of it. `refine_creative_plan` makes a surgical edit to just that one component, landing an identity-driven line exactly where the box opens, without propagating a single change anywhere else in the live plan.

Hook trigger: **identity** — "you get it" recognition, aimed at the parent holding the box open.

## Hook variants

1. **Identity:** "I filmed myself unboxing my own product. Even I didn't read the card that's supposed to say who it's for."
2. **Recognition:** "Forty cents of print, and I bet you can't tell me what your own insert card actually says."
3. **Permission:** "You don't need to rebuild your whole funnel. You need to fix the one card nobody reads."

## Production notes (Higgsfield)

- **Mode:** Per-scene precision — the ad mixes a real unboxing performance (1-2), a live-plan screen state (3), a tool-edit screen state (4-5), and a comparison mock (6); each beat needs individual control rather than one storyboard panel.
- **Reference kit:** Persona character sheet for the founder-actor (same face, kitchen setting, wardrobe throughout); real product photo of the baby carrier and its actual packaging; screen-UI mock of the coach dashboard and the `refine_creative_plan` single-component diff view.
- **Negative prompt / no-text-in-frame:** No legible dashboard chrome, QR codes, or card copy baked into generated frames beyond flat reference — every TEXT ON SCREEN line is added as a post overlay. On-screen disclosure required: "Actor portrayal. Composite scenario for illustration." — never implied as a verified buyer.
- **Diegetic audio:** Kitchen-counter room tone with packaging-tear and paper rustle under scenes 1-2, trackpad clicks under scenes 3-5, no music bed until the end-card sting.
