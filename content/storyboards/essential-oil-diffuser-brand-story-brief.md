## Strategy

"I typed 'fix the brand story, make it feel more premium' into Slack and got back
something worse" is the felt pain — a vague note with no spec behind it, handed to a
VA who has to guess. The diagnosis lives at **amazon_brand_story**: a customer just
said out loud what confused her there, and that specific confusion is exactly what
`generate_brief` needs as input to produce a designer-ready spec instead of another
one-line note. The hook plays **momentum** — the fix ships the same day the finding
comes in, not whenever the VA gets around to guessing right.

## Hook variants

- **momentum** — "I found the exact thing confusing customers on my brand story page — and had a fix brief by lunch."
- **recognition** — "She said out loud the thing I'd been sensing for months and couldn't name."
- **permission** — "I stopped writing vague notes to my VA and started handing her a real spec."

## Production notes (Higgsfield)

- **Mode:** marketing-studio preset (UGC review format — testimonial-style talking head, one setup per persona).
- **Reference kit:** two character sheets (the reviewing customer, kept consistent across scenes 1 only; the founder, kept consistent across scenes 2, 4, 5); screen-UI mock of the coach chat interface and the `generate_brief` one-page output (spec sections rendered as placeholder layout blocks, not real product copy).
- **Negative-prompt / no-text-in-frame:** no baked-in on-screen text inside generated video frames — TEXT ON SCREEN is a post overlay; no real Slack or Amazon Seller Central chrome; screen-recording segments use a generic neutral UI skin.
- **Diegetic audio:** quiet home-office room tone, keyboard clicks under scene 2-4, no music bed until the CTA card.

## CTA & measurement

End card: "Free diagnostic. 6 questions. No account." Destination:
**ideabrandcoach.com/diagnostic**. The metric that proves this ad worked:
**click-through rate on the ad's own link** to the diagnostic landing page —
a founder recognizing her own vague-note habit should out-click a generic
"try our AI tool" pitch. Downstream, the metric that proves the fix itself
worked is **sessions-to-orders on amazon_brand_story** — the real cvr lift
once the brief ships, not just this ad's click performance.
