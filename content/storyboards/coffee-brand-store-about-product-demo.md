## Strategy

The felt pain: "People read my story. They just never click back to actually buy the coffee." A composite founder we'll call Théo roasts single-origin coffee and built a Brand Store About page he's proud of — clean, on-brand, "ethically sourced, small-batch" right up top. Views are healthy. The click-through from that page back to his listing sits at zero.

The connection problem lives at `brand_store_about`: it's the story stop between discovery and the buy button — if it doesn't earn a click *through*, the story is the dead end, not the doorway. The coach runs `run_trust_gap` on the page and flags **Distinctive** as the low score dragging everything down — "ethically sourced" is true of half the category and says nothing only Théo could say.

The hook plays **momentum**: a zero-click-through problem reframed as one fixable score, not a rebrand.

## Hook variants

1. **momentum:** "Zero click-throughs isn't a mystery. It's one score, and it's fixable today."
2. **recognition:** "People read my About page. They just never click past it."
3. **permission:** "You don't need a new brand story. You need to fix the one line that's generic."

5. [12-15s] VISUAL: End card — wordmark on dark background. | VO: "Find the score that's costing you the click. Free." | TEXT ON SCREEN: "ideabrandcoach.com/diagnostic"

## Production notes (Higgsfield)

- **Mode:** Per-scene precision — the dashboard, live scorecard, and before/after copy swap are distinct UI states that need controllable transitions.
- **Reference kit:** Founder character sheet for Théo (roastery setting — on camera in scene 1 only; scenes 2–4 stay on the page/screen, and scene 5 is a pure wordmark end card with no founder cutaway); screen-UI mock of the coach's chat and Trust Gap scorecard; a Brand Store About-page mock built from real coffee-bag product photography for the before/after copy card in scenes 2 and 4.
- **Negative prompt / no-text-in-frame:** No baked-in text in generated frames — UI mocks and page copy render clean, captions added as overlay only. No fabricated farm name, certification, or origin claim presented as real — the origin detail is illustrative of this composite founder's scenario, not an asserted sourcing fact.
- **Diegetic audio:** Quiet roastery ambience (light equipment hum, no music), soft typing and click sounds under the screen-record beats, brand sting on the end card.

## CTA & measurement

**End card copy:** "Find the score that's costing you the click — free." **Destination:** ideabrandcoach.com/diagnostic. **Metric that proves the ad worked:** click-through rate from the ad to /diagnostic — the exact behavior the ad is about, so a lift there proves the pitch landed. **Metric that proves the underlying fix worked:** sessions-to-orders once the About page's Distinctive line changes — the ad's CTR confirms attention, sessions-to-orders confirms the page finally converts it.
