## Strategy

The felt pain: *"Five banners. Zero connection."* The founder can point to exactly where a shopper's attention resets — every one of her five A+ banners is its own unrelated ad, stapled together with no throughline.

The funnel position is `amazon_brand_story`: the module that should carry an already-interested shopper from "curious" to "sold." Hers currently resets that shopper's attention five separate times instead of building one case. `generate_aplus_content_plan` restructures it as one scrolling 1472×3008 composition with a beginning, a middle, and an end.

Hook trigger: momentum — this is a same-session fix run live on screen, not a redesign project.

## Hook variants

1. **Momentum** — "Five banners. Zero connection. One coach session to fix it."
2. **Recognition** — "Every one of my five A+ banners resets the shopper's attention to zero."
3. **Permission** — "You don't need a new design agency. You need one continuous story."

## Production notes (Higgsfield)

- **Mode:** storyboard-image default (one multi-panel storyboard image driving one video job — suited to a pure screen-capture UI demo with no on-camera talent).
- **Reference kit:** screen-UI mock of the live A+ content module and the coach's plan-builder output; product sheet built from the real insulated-bottle photo for the composition mockup; no persona character sheet needed for this talent-free demo format.
- **Negative prompt / no-text rule:** no baked-in on-screen text in generated frames beyond the specified overlay; render the seller dashboard as a generic mock, never a trademarked platform screenshot or logo.
- **Diegetic audio:** soft UI cursor-click and scroll SFX under each beat, no music bed until the end card.

## CTA & measurement

**End card copy:** "Stop stapling banners together. Tell one story. Free diagnostic." **Destination:** ideabrandcoach.com/diagnostic. **Metric that proves the ad worked:** click-through rate from this ad to `/diagnostic` — proof the momentum hook (a same-session fix, demonstrated live) moves founders with a visibly disconnected A+ module to check their own. The metric that proves the *fix* worked: sessions-to-orders once the one-composition version replaces the five banners — the cvr movement, not just the click.
