## Strategy

The felt pain, in his words: "I'm ranking page one. That should be a win. But my click-through rate says nobody cares that I'm there." The funnel position is `seo_content` — specifically the meta description, the last few lines a searcher reads before deciding whether to click his result over the three above it. `design_test` solves the connection problem by turning "maybe it's the copy" into a structured, measurable comparison: a features-led variant against a fear-led variant, same window, one metric.

The hook plays **fear_of_loss** — the ad itself demonstrates the trigger it's describing, naming the exact bad day (an accident with no proof) that made one variant win.

## Hook variants

1. **fear_of_loss** — "Page one. Barely any clicks. Turns out the fear was in the wrong sentence."
2. **momentum** — "We didn't rewrite the page. We rewrote two lines and ran a test."
3. **recognition** — "If you're ranking well and getting ignored, this is probably why."

## Production notes (Higgsfield)

- **Mode:** storyboard-image (5 scenes, screen-recording-driven product_demo format; a single multi-panel storyboard image covers the flat compositional needs here, no live-action performer required).
- **Reference kit:** screen-UI mock of the `design_test` two-variant comparison panel and the resulting CTR comparison card, built from the real in-app layout; a browser-chrome reference frame for the Search Console and CMS screens to keep visual consistency across scenes 1-4.
- **Negative prompt / no-text-in-frame:** no baked-in captions or logos inside the generated screen-mock plates beyond the actual UI copy already specified — all TEXT ON SCREEN overlay lines are added in post, not generated.
- **Diegetic audio:** subtle keyboard/mouse-click sounds under scenes 2 and 4; a light "data reveal" tone under the comparison card in scene 3; near-silent otherwise so VO carries the explanation.

## CTA & measurement

- **End card copy:** "Test the line before you trust the rank." + ideabrandcoach.com/diagnostic
- **Destination:** ideabrandcoach.com/diagnostic
- **Metric that proves the ad worked:** click-through to `/diagnostic` from viewers who watched through the scene 3 results reveal — confirms it's the "here's the number that proved it" moment driving conversion, not just the SEO-founder pain recognition.
