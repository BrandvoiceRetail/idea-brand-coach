## Strategy

Her felt pain: *"I paid for a whole new photoshoot for my A+ content. Conversion didn't move an inch."* She assumed the problem was visual polish and spent budget on better lighting and styling, convinced that was the fix.

The funnel position is `amazon_brand_story`: the module meant to carry an already-interested shopper into "sold" with one continuous case for the brand. Hers stayed five disconnected promotional banners regardless of how good the new photos looked — new pictures inside the same broken structure. `generate_aplus_content_plan` reframes the module as one continuous editorial composition across 5 addressable beats, using photos she already had.

Hook trigger: momentum — the fix isn't visual, it's structural, and it happens in one coach session, not another shoot.

## Hook variants

1. **Momentum** — "I paid for a new photoshoot. The conversion number didn't move an inch."
2. **Recognition** — "New photos, same five disconnected banners. Of course nothing changed."
3. **Permission** — "You're allowed to stop blaming the photography."
