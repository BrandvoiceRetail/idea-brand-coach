## Strategy

"I've said 'add trust signals' in three different Slack messages and nothing ever
shipped" is the felt pain — a note vague enough that no VA or designer could act
on it, so it just sat there for months. The diagnosis and the fix both land at
**trust_badges_social_proof**, the buy-box moment where a hesitating shopper looks
for permission to click "buy": `generate_brief` turns the coach's finding into an
exact, placeable spec — which badge, which line, which position near the button —
instead of another repeat of the same vague note. The hook plays **permission** —
the shopper at the buy box needs a specific signal, and so did the founder's own
team, before either one could act.

## Hook variants

- **permission** — "I said 'add trust signals' three times. Nothing shipped until the coach got specific."
- **momentum** — "Months of the same Slack note, fixed the moment I had an exact spec instead of a vibe."
- **recognition** — "I knew something was missing near my buy box. I just never knew what to call it."

## Production notes (Higgsfield)

- **Mode:** per-scene precision (founder-story format — six distinct beats across workshop and desk settings benefit from individually directed shots over a single storyboard image).
- **Reference kit:** persona character sheet for the founder (consistent face, workshop wardrobe, and desk setting across all 6 scenes); screen-UI mock of the coach's trust_badges_social_proof finding and the `generate_brief` placeable spec (badge/line/position mockup as placeholder layout, not real Amazon buy-box chrome).
- **Negative-prompt / no-text-in-frame:** no baked-in on-screen text inside generated video frames — all TEXT ON SCREEN is a post overlay; no real Amazon Seller Central or Slack UI rendered; no fabricated badge or certification claims shown as real.
- **Diegetic audio:** workshop room tone (light background hum) on scenes 1 and 4-5, keyboard/click foley under the screen-recording beats in scenes 2-3, no music bed until the CTA card.
