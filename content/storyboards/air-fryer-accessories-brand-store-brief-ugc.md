## Strategy

"I keep telling my designer 'just make it better'" is the felt pain — the founder
knows the About page isn't working but has never had language specific enough to
hand off. The diagnosis and the fix both live at **brand_store_about**: a shopper
names exactly what she wanted to see and didn't, and `generate_brief` turns that
real gap into a concrete, placeable spec instead of another round of "make it
pop." The hook plays **permission** — she's allowed to stop guessing at vague
direction and hand over something specific.

## Hook variants

- **permission** — "I finally have something concrete to hand my designer, instead of 'just make it better.'"
- **recognition** — "A shopper told me exactly what she wanted from my About page. I'd never asked."
- **momentum** — "One gap named, one brief generated, one message sent to my designer. Today."

## Scene-by-scene

1. [0-3s] VISUAL: Shopper on camera, casual selfie framing, thinking back on browsing the brand's storefront. | VO: "I clicked their About page hoping for a reason to trust them. Nothing." | TEXT ON SCREEN: "Real shopper feedback"
2. [3-6s] VISUAL: Cut to founder at her desk, screen-recording as she feeds that exact feedback into the coach and the finding lands on brand_store_about. | VO: "That's the gap the coach confirmed on my About page." | TEXT ON SCREEN: "brand_store_about: gap confirmed"
3. [6-9s] VISUAL: `generate_brief` output builds on screen — a concrete spec with copy direction and layout notes replacing a blank placeholder page. | VO: "So instead of another vague note, I had an actual brief to hand off." | TEXT ON SCREEN: "generate_brief → concrete spec"
4. [9-12s] VISUAL: Founder forwards the brief to her designer in one message, no extra explanation needed. | VO: "Months of 'meaning to redo it,' solved in one message." | TEXT ON SCREEN: "Sent."
5. [12-15s] VISUAL: Founder to camera, closing her laptop. | VO: "Start with the free diagnostic." | TEXT ON SCREEN: "ideabrandcoach.com/diagnostic"

## Production notes (Higgsfield)

- **Mode:** marketing-studio preset (UGC review format — two-persona testimonial-into-demo structure).
- **Reference kit:** character sheet for the shopper (scene 1 only, kept distinct from the founder); character sheet for the founder (scenes 2, 4, 5); screen-UI mock of the coach finding and the `generate_brief` output (placeholder spec content, not real Amazon Brand Store screenshots).
- **Negative-prompt / no-text-in-frame:** no baked-in on-screen text inside generated video frames — TEXT ON SCREEN is a post overlay only; no real Amazon Seller Central UI chrome; no fabricated brief content presented as an actual customer's brand copy.
- **Diegetic audio:** quiet living-room tone on scene 1, desk/keyboard foley under the screen-recording beats, no music bed until the CTA card.

## CTA & measurement

End card: "Free diagnostic. 6 questions. No account." Destination:
**ideabrandcoach.com/diagnostic**. The metric that proves this ad worked:
**click-through rate on the ad's own link** to the diagnostic landing page —
a founder recognizing her own "meaning to redo it" stall should out-click a
generic tool-feature pitch. Downstream, once the brief ships, the metric that
proves the fix itself worked is **sessions-to-orders on brand_store_about** —
the real cvr lift, not just this ad's own click rate.
