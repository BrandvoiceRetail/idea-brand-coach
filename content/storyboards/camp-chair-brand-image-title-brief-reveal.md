## Strategy

"My ACOS has crept up for two months and I haven't touched a single bid" is the
felt pain — spend is buying the same clicks it always did, but each one costs more,
and nothing about the campaign setup explains why. The diagnosis lives at
**amazon_main_image**, the exact scroll-decision moment in the search grid: if the
image and title aren't earning the click, rising CPCs just make the leak more
expensive, not more visible. `generate_main_image_title_plan` fixes the connection
problem by forcing image and title into one positioning statement instead of
another vague "make it pop" note. The hook plays **momentum** — reframing a
two-month erosion into a same-week, one-page fix, not a redesign project.

## Hook variants

- **momentum** — "My ACOS climbed for two months without me touching a single bid, so I opened a one-page plan instead."
- **permission** — "I know it's one page and eleven words, but I didn't need a whole rebrand to fix this."
- **recognition** — "The plan came back and named the exact thing I'd been avoiding — my title and my image were saying two different things."

## Production notes (Higgsfield)

- **Mode:** marketing-studio preset (UGC unboxing format — persona-led, handheld pacing).
- **Reference kit:** persona character sheet for the founder (consistent face, kitchen setting, wardrobe across all 5 scenes); screen-UI mock of the exported Main Image + Title Plan page (before/after image comparison + title-formula breakdown) built as a printable mock, not a real Amazon screenshot.
- **Negative-prompt / no-text-in-frame:** no baked-in on-page text rendered inside the generated video frames — all TEXT ON SCREEN is added as a post overlay; no real Amazon UI chrome; no illegible or garbled placeholder copy on the printed page prop; the ACOS figure is framed as this composite founder's illustrative scenario, never presented as a verified real result.
- **Diegetic audio:** paper-flip and page-set-down foley, quiet kitchen room tone, single handheld take; no music bed until the CTA card.

## CTA & measurement

End card: "Free diagnostic. 6 questions. No account." Destination:
**ideabrandcoach.com/diagnostic**. The one metric that proves this ad worked:
**click-through rate on the ad's own link** to the diagnostic landing page —
naming the exact fix on-camera should out-click a generic "try our tool" pitch.
