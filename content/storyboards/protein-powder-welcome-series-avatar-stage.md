## Strategy

The felt pain, in his words: *"My welcome email says 'thanks for your order.' That's it. That's the whole thing."* It could go to anyone, which means it doesn't really land with anyone — a plant-based athlete, a new vegan, and a post-workout mom all get the same flat line.

The funnel position is `welcome_series`. `build_avatar_stage` runs the forensic four-stage build — S1 vocabulary → S2 job map → S3 triggers → S4 objections — against his actual customers, surfacing the real words they use and the real reason they bought, so day one finally sounds like it knows who's reading it.

The hook runs on **recognition** — the relief of feeling understood on day one, instead of processed.

## Hook variants

1. **Recognition:** "My welcome email didn't know who bought. Turns out, neither did I."
2. **Identity:** "Anyone could have written my welcome email. That was the problem."
3. **Belonging:** "Day one, and my email already felt like a stranger sent it."
