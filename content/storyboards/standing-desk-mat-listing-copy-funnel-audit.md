## Strategy

The felt pain, in her words: "I spent a month convinced my ad targeting was broken. I kept narrowing audiences, kept cutting 'wasted' impressions, and my conversion rate barely moved." She did the thing every founder does when CVR is flat — blame the traffic. Nobody told her to look downstream of the click instead.

The specific funnel position is `amazon_listing_copy`. `run_funnel_audit` overlays her customer avatar across every touchpoint in her funnel — ad creative, listing, brand story, reviews, buy box — and names the single weakest link: her `amazon_listing_copy` never addresses the actual reason her avatar hesitates (whether the mat holds up on hardwood over a full workday). Everything downstream of the click was fine. The ad targeting was never the problem.

Hook trigger: **momentum** — the audit doesn't hand her a full rebuild, it hands her one weak link and a reason to fix exactly that today.

## Hook variants

1. **momentum:** "I spent a month fixing my ad targeting. The audit took ninety seconds to find the real problem."
2. **fear_of_loss:** "I kept paying to narrow my targeting. The leak was three steps past the click, the whole time."
3. **permission:** "You don't have to guess which touchpoint is broken. You're allowed to just ask."

## Production notes (Higgsfield)

- **Mode:** Per-scene precision. The ads-dashboard beat (scenes 1-2) and the funnel-audit overlay UI (scenes 4-5) are distinct mock states that need to stay legible and consistent across cuts — storyboard-image mode would blur the dashboard-to-audit transition.
- **Reference kit:** Persona character sheet for the founder-actor (same face, home-office setting, wardrobe throughout); product reference sheet for the standing-desk mat from real product photography; screen-UI mocks of the ads dashboard (scenes 1-2) and the coach's funnel-audit overlay with per-touchpoint scores (scenes 4-5).
- **Negative prompt / no-text-in-frame:** Do not render legible dashboard numbers, chart labels, or audit UI copy inside generated frames — supply all dashboard and audit states as flat reference images and add every TEXT ON SCREEN line as a post overlay. On-screen disclosure required: "Actor portrayal. Composite scenario for illustration."
- **Diegetic audio:** Quiet home-office room tone throughout; a frustrated exhale under scene 2; trackpad clicks under scenes 3-5; a slight tonal lift (single soft chime, no music bed) on the "weakest link" reveal in scene 4.
