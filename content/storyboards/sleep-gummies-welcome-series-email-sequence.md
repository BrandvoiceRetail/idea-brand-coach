## Strategy

The felt pain, in the founder's words: *"The order confirmation goes out. That's it. That's the whole relationship."* Buyers place one order and disappear — no second purchase, no habit, nothing. She's been treating it as a product problem when it's a silence problem: nobody ever spoke to them again after the receipt.

The funnel position is `welcome_series`: the retention sequence meant to turn a first-time buyer into someone building a routine, not a one-off. Hers is a single confirmation email and then nothing. `create_email_sequence` builds the full 5-step welcome series, opening step one on the actual fear that matters for a sleep product — that the problem creeps back in if nothing changes.

The hook runs on **fear_of_loss** — naming what actually happens if the buyer does nothing, instead of just saying "thanks for your order."

## Hook variants

1. **Fear_of_loss:** "If nothing changes, the sleep problem just creeps back in a few weeks — and I never told anyone that."
2. **Recognition:** "Order confirmation email. Then silence. Forever."
3. **Momentum:** "It didn't take a redesign. It took five emails and one session."

## Production notes (Higgsfield)

- **Mode:** Per-scene precision — screen-capture-led problem/solution demo (real dashboard mock, real sequence-builder UI) requiring exact control scene by scene.
- **Reference kit:** Founder character sheet for any on-camera cutaway; screen-UI mocks of a generic email-flow dashboard, a repeat-purchase chart, and the coach's `create_email_sequence` 5-step timeline output; real product photo of the sleep-gummy bottle/jar.
- **Negative prompt / no-text rule:** No text, labels, or captions baked into generated UI-mock frames — every caption and email-subject line is a post-production overlay per the TEXT ON SCREEN field; do not fabricate specific email copy beyond what's specified, and render dashboards as generic mocks, never a trademarked platform screenshot.
- **Diegetic audio:** Quiet home-office room tone, trackpad and keyboard clicks timed to the tool run, a soft UI-render chime as the 5-step timeline appears, no music bed until the end-card sting.
- **Edit-before-regenerate:** if the 5-step timeline mock needs a wording pass, edit that generation directly rather than re-rendering the dashboard-to-timeline sequence from scratch.
