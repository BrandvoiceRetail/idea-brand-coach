## Strategy

His own words: "I've got the ad funnel mapped, the email sequence mapped. Founder social? We just... post when we remember."

The funnel position is `founder_social` — every other position in his funnel has a defined asset and a plan behind it, and this one runs on whatever he feels like posting that week. It's not that founder social is weak; it's that it's not actually a position in his funnel at all — it's an afterthought wearing a funnel position's name. `get_funnel_coverage` makes the gap visible by showing it sitting empty next to five positions that are covered, so "we post sometimes" stops passing as a strategy.

Hook trigger: recognition. He's not being told founder social is bad — he's being shown it was never actually planned.
