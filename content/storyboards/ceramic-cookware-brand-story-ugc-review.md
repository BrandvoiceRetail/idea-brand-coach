## Strategy

The felt pain, in a genuinely interested shopper's own words: *"I scrolled her whole brand story section and still don't know anything I didn't already know."* The founder was proud of the new photography in her A+ content — she never checked whether it said anything new.

The funnel position is `amazon_brand_story`: where an interested-but-undecided shopper looks for the reason beyond the listing bullets to actually buy. Hers restated the same "oven-safe ceramic" claim across all five slides in a different font each time. `generate_aplus_content_plan`'s 5 addressable beats give the module new content that page never had.

Hook trigger: recognition — the shopper names the exact gap the founder couldn't see from inside her own listing.

## Hook variants

1. **Recognition** — "I scrolled her whole brand story and didn't learn one new thing."
2. **Identity** — "Pretty photos of a pan don't tell me if it's for someone like me."
3. **Momentum** — "The fix wasn't a new photoshoot. It was five new beats."

## Production notes (Higgsfield)

- **Mode:** marketing-studio UGC preset (review format — two-persona continuity between the shopper's monologue and the founder's screen-insert cutaway).
- **Reference kit:** two character sheets (shopper persona, founder persona) held consistent across their respective shots; product sheet built from the real ceramic-cookware photo; screen-UI mock of the coach's A+ plan output for scenes 3-4.
- **Negative prompt / no-text rule:** no baked-in on-screen text in generated frames; do not render invented certification badges or star-rating graphics on the phone-screen inserts beyond what's specified.
- **Diegetic audio:** light room ambience under both monologues, a soft phone-scroll SFX in scene 2, no music until the end card.
