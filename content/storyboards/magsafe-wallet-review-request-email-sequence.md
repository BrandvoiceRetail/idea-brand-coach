## Strategy

His own words: "The review request fires the second the order ships. Nobody's even used the thing yet. Of course they ignore it."

The funnel position is `review_request_flow` — the ask is timed to the shipping event, not to the moment the customer has actually lived with the product. A MagSafe wallet only proves itself once someone's dropped their card in it for a week and it hasn't fallen off. Asking on day one means asking a stranger for a favor before they have anything to say. `create_email_sequence` rebuilds the ask around a real usage window, so the request lands after the product has earned an opinion, not before.

Hook trigger: permission. He's not chasing more reviews — he's earning the right to ask for one.

## Hook variants

1. **Permission** — "Turns out I didn't have to ask for a review on day one."
2. **Recognition** — "My review request went out on day one. Nobody's opened the box by then."
3. **Momentum** — "One sequence change, and the ask actually lands when it should."
