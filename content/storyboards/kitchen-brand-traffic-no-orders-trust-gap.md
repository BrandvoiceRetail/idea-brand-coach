## Strategy

Her felt pain, in her own words: *"1,200 sessions this week. Nine orders. A video did the hard part — getting people here — and my listing still can't close."* A viral clip tripled her traffic overnight; she assumed the flat order count meant her price was wrong, because that's the only lever she knew to blame.

The funnel position is `amazon_listing_copy`: the page doing the actual selling once a shopper lands, past the click, past the image. `run_trust_gap` scans that copy and names **Authentic** as the weakest of the four IDEA pillars — the bullets read like they were copied off a spec sheet, not written by someone who's actually used a stand mixer.

The hook runs on **recognition** — she already senses something's off (a traffic spike that doesn't convert always feels wrong), and the ad's job is to name the exact thing instead of leaving her to guess at price.

## Hook variants

1. **Recognition:** "1,200 sessions this week. Nine orders. Something's off, and it's not the price."
2. **Fear_of_loss:** "Every one of those 1,200 sessions? A shopper I paid for. Lost anyway."
3. **Permission:** "Forget the discount — go find out which pillar of your listing is actually failing."

## Production notes (Higgsfield)

- **Mode:** Per-scene precision — this is a screen-capture-led demo (real analytics dashboard, real coach UI, real bullet text) that needs exact control scene by scene, not one multi-panel storyboard image.
- **Reference kit:** Consistent desk/monitor setup and coach-UI chrome across scenes 2-4 (same browser frame, same cursor style) so the screen-capture reads as one continuous session; screen-UI mock of the Amazon Business Reports session graph; screen-UI mock of the coach's chat and Trust Gap scorecard component.
- **Negative prompt / no-text rule:** No text, labels, or captions baked into the generated frames — dashboard numbers and UI must render clean; every caption in this storyboard is a post-production overlay per the TEXT ON SCREEN field. Do not hallucinate extra metrics, badges, or logos onto the analytics mock.
- **Diegetic audio:** Soft trackpad taps and mouse-click sounds timed to cursor movement, quiet home-office room tone, no music bed until the end-card sting.

## CTA & measurement

**End card copy:** "Run your Trust Gap score — free." **Destination:** ideabrandcoach.com/diagnostic. **Metric that proves the ad worked:** click-through rate from the ad to /diagnostic proves the hook landed; sessions-to-orders on the listing is the metric that proves the fix itself worked — the ad's whole thesis is that a traffic spike without a diagnosis is wasted, so watching that ratio recover once the bullets get rewritten is the real payoff, not just the click.
