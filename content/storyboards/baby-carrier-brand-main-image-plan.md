## Strategy

The felt pain, in her words: "A video went semi-viral. Impressions were solid. The clicks never showed up." A TikTok mention did the awareness job for her, but the listing itself wasn't ready to convert that attention — her main image was making the generic case ("ergonomic") instead of the specific one that actually earns a click at the scroll.

The specific funnel position is `amazon_main_image`. `generate_main_image_title_plan` gives her one main-image-and-title positioning statement plus a two-variant CTR split-test, so she can see which specific detail — not the generic claim — actually earns the click.

Hook trigger: **belonging** — the winning concept isn't the cleanest studio shot, it's the one that looks like it was made for a body like the shopper's.

## Hook variants

1. **belonging:** "The winning image wasn't the cleanest shot. It was the one that looked like it was made for a body like yours."
2. **fear_of_loss:** "A video went semi-viral. The clicks never showed up. That's money I left on the table."
3. **momentum:** "One split test told me more than three studio shoots ever did."

## Production notes (Higgsfield)

- **Mode:** Marketing-studio preset (UGC try-on). The on-body try-on beat with a phone-in-hand screen-share cutaway is the preset's target shape.
- **Reference kit:** Persona character sheet for the founder-actor (same face, wardrobe, home setting throughout); product reference sheet for the carrier from the real product photo; a clean mock-UI reference of the Ads dashboard and the split-test plan card for scenes 2-4.
- **Negative prompt / no-text-in-frame:** Do not render legible dashboard labels or plan UI copy inside the frame — supply those as flat reference images and add every TEXT ON SCREEN line as a post overlay. On-screen disclosure required: "Actor portrayal. Composite scenario for illustration." — a fictional founder persona, never implied as a verified buyer.
- **Diegetic audio:** Home-living-room room tone throughout; a soft carrier-buckle click under scene 1; a phone-unlock tap under scene 2; light trackpad/swipe sounds under scenes 3-4; VO is one continuous take, not narration over music.
