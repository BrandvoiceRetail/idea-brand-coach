## Strategy

Her felt pain: *"Impressions climbing every month. CTR on the same queries quietly dropping."* The top-line traffic chart still looks fine, which is exactly why this leak goes unnoticed for months — nobody double-checks a number that's trending up.

The funnel position is `seo_content`: the same ranking post competing in a search-results snippet that three other posts now answer with the identical "natural ingredients" claim, so distinctiveness in the preview text — not the ranking — is what's leaking.

The hook runs on **momentum** — the fix is one line of meta copy today, not another 1,500-word rewrite next quarter.

## Hook variants

1. **Recognition:** "Impressions climbing every month. CTR on the same queries quietly dropping."
2. **Momentum:** "Fixing this wasn't another 1,500-word rewrite. It was one line of meta copy."
3. **Fear_of_loss:** "Every month I didn't check this, I was losing clicks I'd already earned in rank."

## Production notes (Higgsfield)

- **Mode:** Per-scene precision — a screen-share walkthrough of two separate dashboards (Search Console, then the coach) needs exact frame-by-frame control rather than one storyboard panel.
- **Reference kit:** Screen-UI mock of the Search Console impressions/CTR charts; screen-UI mock of the coach's chat and Trust Gap scorecard; a real screenshot of the ranking blog post for continuity.
- **Negative prompt / no-text rule:** No text or numbers baked into the generated UI mocks beyond what's specified — avoid hallucinated chart labels or dates. All overlay copy is added in post per the TEXT ON SCREEN field.
- **Diegetic audio:** Quiet home-office room tone, trackpad/mouse-click sounds timed to cursor movement, no music bed until the end-card sting.

## CTA & measurement

**End card copy:** "Healthy traffic, declining clicks? Find the pillar that's leaking. Free diagnostic." **Destination:** ideabrandcoach.com/diagnostic. **Metric that proves the ad worked:** click-through rate from the ad to /diagnostic — proof the momentum hook earned the tap. The real-world payoff for a founder who runs the fix: CTR recovery on the target queries (Search Console CTR delta), the same number the ad opens on.
