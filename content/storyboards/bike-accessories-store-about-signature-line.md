## Strategy

His felt pain, in his own words: *"My About page tells you I built this in my garage. It never tells you why that should matter to you."* The origin story is all there — the garage, the first prototype welded on a workbench — and none of it answers the one question a shopper actually has: what's different about this one.

The funnel position is `brand_store_about`: the module built to carry a curious visitor from "nice story" to "I trust this brand enough to buy." His stayed a timeline of facts with no distinguishing claim anywhere in it. `generate_signature` turns the story he's already telling into the one sentence that states the difference — the line he can say on camera and pin at the top of the page.

Hook trigger: identity — the fix isn't a better story, it's the sentence that was missing from the one he's already telling.

## Hook variants

1. **Identity** — "My About page tells you I started this in a garage. It never tells you why that matters to you."
2. **Recognition** — "Origin story: check. The one sentence that makes you buy: missing."
3. **Fear_of_loss** — "Every visitor who reads my whole story and still can't tell me what's different — that's a sale I talked myself out of."

## Production notes (Higgsfield)

- **Mode:** Storyboard-image default — one continuous garage setting across a calm personal narrative; a single multi-panel storyboard image drives one video job.
- **Reference kit:** Founder character sheet for continuity across all six shots; product reference sheet built from real handlebar-mount and phone-holder photos for the pegboard dressing; screen-UI mocks of the Brand Store About page and the coach's `generate_signature` output card.
- **Negative prompt / no-text-in-frame:** No baked-in on-screen copy inside generated frames — every TEXT ON SCREEN line, including the DM and the signature line, is composited in post; render the coach UI as a clean generic mock, not a trademarked screenshot.
- **Diegetic audio:** Garage room tone throughout; a light tool-clink under scene 1; phone-notification tap under scene 3; quiet laptop-key sounds under scene 4; no music bed until the end card.
