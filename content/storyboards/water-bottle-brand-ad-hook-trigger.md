## Strategy

The felt pain, in the founder's own words: "I reshot this ad three times. New footage, new lighting, same flat number." A composite founder we'll call Jess sells an insulated water bottle. Four edits deep, she kept assuming the problem was production quality — better b-roll, better cuts.

The connection problem lives at `paid_social_creative`: the scroll-past moment in the feed. Every edit kept leading with the same content — double-wall steel, 24-hour cold retention — spec talk. The coach runs `identify_decision_trigger` and the real lever isn't the product spec at all, it's **belonging**: her buyers want to look like the kind of person who already has their water bottle sorted, not sit through an insulation lesson.

The hook plays that exact trigger back at the viewer, addressing the scroller directly rather than pitching the bottle.

## Hook variants

1. **belonging:** "Everyone at the gym already has one. You're just not carrying it yet."
2. **identity:** "You didn't want a bottle that keeps water cold. You wanted people to think you've got your life together."
3. **momentum:** "Don't shoot a fifth edit. Fix the first line."

## Production notes (Higgsfield)

- **Mode:** per-scene — this founder_story format needs Jess's face and setting to hold steady across the confessional beats (1–3) before cutting to a distinct new-concept mockup (scene 4), so per-scene precision beats a single storyboard image here.
- **Reference kit:** founder character sheet for Jess (same face, same loungewear/setting across scenes 1–3); product reference sheet built from the real bottle photos for both the old-edit montage and the new locker-room concept; a screen-UI mock of the coach's trigger-result card locked as a reference asset.
- **Negative prompt / no-text-in-frame:** no baked-in captions, lower-thirds, or UI labels in any generated frame — all on-screen copy is a separate overlay layer added in post.
- **Diegetic audio:** ambient room tone under scenes 1–3 (Jess talking to camera, handheld); a locker-room ambience bed (light chatter, gym echo) under scene 4's new-concept mockup; brand sting under the scene 5 CTA card.

## CTA & measurement

- **End card copy:** "Find the trigger before you reshoot again." with the ideabrandcoach.com wordmark.
- **Destination:** ideabrandcoach.com/diagnostic
- **The one metric that proves the ad worked:** click-through rate on the ad itself — the ad's own hook-swap story only lands if it earns more clicks than a spec-led opener would.
