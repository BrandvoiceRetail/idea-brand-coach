## Strategy

Her own words: "We repositioned the brand three months ago. My own bio still says the old line. I just never went back and fixed it."

The funnel position is `organic_social_profile` — bio, pinned posts, highlights — is the first thing a curious follower checks after seeing one good post, and right now it's telling them a story the rest of the brand abandoned months ago. A repositioning that doesn't reach every live surface isn't a repositioning, it's a rumor. `refine_creative_plan` is built for exactly this: propagate a positioning change across every live asset in one pass, instead of a founder manually hunting down every old bio line and pinned caption.

Hook trigger: identity. The mismatch isn't cosmetic — it's the brand telling two different people who it is.

## Hook variants

1. **Identity** — "My brand repositioned three months ago. My bio's still living in the old one."
2. **Recognition** — "We changed everything about the brand except the one place people check first."
3. **Momentum** — "One pass fixed every old caption I was dreading going back to update."

## Production notes (Higgsfield)

- **Mode:** Storyboard-image (single continuous founder-at-home setting; five beats render as one multi-panel storyboard image into one video job).
- **Reference kit:** Persona character sheet (same founder, same setting, same wardrobe across all five shots); product reference sheet for the air fryer accessory from the real product photo; screen-UI mock of the coach chat, the refine plan output, and the before/after checklist for scenes 3-4.
- **Negative prompt / no-text-in-frame:** No legible bio text, caption copy, or checklist labels rendered inside generated frames — supply the old-bio and checklist views as flat reference mocks; every TEXT ON SCREEN line is a post overlay.
- **Diegetic audio:** Quiet room ambience under scenes 1-2; a soft notification-style chime as each checklist item marks updated in scene 4; no music bed.

## CTA & measurement

End card copy: "If your brand changed and your profile didn't, find out what else is stale. Free diagnostic, 6 questions, no account." Destination: `ideabrandcoach.com/diagnostic`. The one metric that proves this ad worked: **CTR to /diagnostic among viewers who saw the identity-mismatch hook (scene 1)** — proof the recognition of a stale profile is what moves someone to check their own.
