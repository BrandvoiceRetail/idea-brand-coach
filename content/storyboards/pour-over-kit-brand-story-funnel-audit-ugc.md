## Strategy

The felt pain, from a real buyer's mouth: "Honestly? The ad's what got me. Then the listing sealed it — clear, specific, exactly what I wanted to know. Then I scrolled to the brand story part and just... left." That's the moment a founder never sees, because he's not the one scrolling his own funnel as a stranger would.

The specific funnel position is `amazon_brand_story` — a module this founder hadn't touched in over a year, confident it was fine because nobody had complained. `run_funnel_audit`'s weakest-link overlay scores every touchpoint against his real customer avatar and flags `amazon_brand_story` exactly where this buyer dropped off — everything before it worked.

Hook trigger: **recognition** — the page he never thought to check was the one actually losing people.

## Hook variants

1. **recognition:** "The ad worked. The listing worked. Then I got to the brand story and just closed the tab."
2. **fear_of_loss:** "Everything before that page convinced me. One page undid all of it."
3. **momentum:** "One page. That's the whole fix — not a funnel rebuild."
