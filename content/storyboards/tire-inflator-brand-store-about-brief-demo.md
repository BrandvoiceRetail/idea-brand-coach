## Strategy

"I've had 'fix the About page' on my to-do list for four months" is the felt pain —
not a hard problem, just a never-prioritized one, because there was never a
concrete first step. The diagnosis and the fix both land at **brand_store_about**:
the coach names the specific miss in one pass, then `generate_brief` turns that
finding into an exact, handoff-ready spec — copy, layout, image direction — in the
same working session. The hook plays **momentum** — the barrier wasn't difficulty,
it was never having a first step small enough to actually start.

## Hook variants

- **momentum** — "This was on my to-do list for four months. It took one session to actually move."
- **permission** — "I didn't need to redesign my whole Brand Store. I needed one page and a brief."
- **recognition** — "The coach named the exact reason my About page wasn't converting, and I'd walked past it a hundred times."

## Production notes (Higgsfield)

- **Mode:** storyboard-image (screen-recording product demo — single multi-panel storyboard image driving one video job for pacing consistency).
- **Reference kit:** persona character sheet for the founder (brief on-camera appearance, scenes 1 and 4-5 only); screen-UI mock of the coach's finding output and the `generate_brief` one-page spec (placeholder copy/layout blocks, not real Amazon Brand Store chrome).
- **Negative-prompt / no-text-in-frame:** no baked-in on-screen text inside generated video frames — all TEXT ON SCREEN is a post overlay; no real Amazon Seller Central or Brand Store UI rendered; placeholder brief content only, never legible fabricated product claims.
- **Diegetic audio:** soft cursor-click and keyboard foley under the screen-recording beats, quiet room tone on camera cutaways, no music bed until the CTA card.

## CTA & measurement

End card: "Free diagnostic. 6 questions. No account." Destination:
**ideabrandcoach.com/diagnostic**. The metric that proves this ad worked:
**click-through rate on the ad's own link** to the diagnostic landing page —
the "stuck to-do, solved in one sitting" framing should out-click a generic
feature-list pitch. Downstream, the metric that proves the fix itself worked
is **sessions-to-orders on brand_store_about** — the About page actually
converting browsers, not just this ad's click performance.
