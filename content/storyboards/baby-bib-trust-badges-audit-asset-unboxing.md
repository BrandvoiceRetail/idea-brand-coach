## Strategy

Her own words: "I was filming a routine unboxing for content. I wasn't even looking for a problem." Mid-unboxing, on camera, she noticed something she'd walked past a hundred times: no visible safety-certification mark anywhere near the buy box — the exact signal an anxious first-time parent goes looking for before they'll trust silicone near their baby's mouth.

The specific funnel position is `trust_badges_social_proof`. `audit_asset` scores that trust-badge asset against her customer avatar and confirms it: the missing certification mark is precisely the signal her avatar searches for near the buy box, not a nice-to-have.

Hook trigger: **fear_of_loss** — every day that badge is missing, the most anxious parent in her funnel is the one who leaves.
