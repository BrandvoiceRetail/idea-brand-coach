## Summary of Changes

The revised methodology implements Trevor's evidence-pattern-interpretation-response chain:

1. **JTBD Removal:** All references to "job map", "hiring the product", and similar IP-protected terms replaced with "THE CHANGE THEY ARE SEEKING" and "INFERRED DESIRED STATE"

2. **Trust Signal Logic:** Stage 4 resolutions now follow the chain: identify trust requirement → specify evidence the brand CAN provide (not promises they may not offer)

3. **Evidence Classes:** Clear distinction between:
   - CUSTOMER EVIDENCE (verbatim quotes only)
   - PATTERN DETECTED (observed from multiple signals)
   - IDEA INTERPRETATION (framework-based analysis)
   - RECOMMENDED RESPONSE (tactical execution)

4. **Inference Language:** Model-generated interpretations use "suggests", "likely", "may", "appears" rather than stating as observed fact

5. **UK English:** No em dashes, UK spelling throughout

The core forensic methodology remains intact while the framing explicitly shows the interpretive chain from evidence to recommendation.
