# Quick Start: Create Your First Avatar in 15 Minutes

## Purpose
Get you creating your first customer avatar quickly using the book-guided chat workflow.

## What You'll Accomplish
By the end of this guide, you'll have:
- Created your first customer avatar
- Learned the IDEA framework basics
- Set up performance tracking
- Generated a strategy document

## Step 1: Start Your First Avatar (2 minutes)

### Desktop
1. Click "Create Avatar" or the **+** tab
2. Name your avatar (e.g., "Weekend Warrior")
3. Choose a template or start from scratch

### Mobile
1. Tap the **+** icon in the avatar tabs
2. Enter avatar name
3. Select template or blank start

> 💡 **Tip**: Use descriptive names like "Budget-Conscious Mom" or "Tech-Savvy Millennial" instead of "Avatar 1"

## Step 2: Book-Guided Conversation (10 minutes)

The chat will guide you through key chapters from "What Captures The Heart Goes In The Cart". Answer naturally - the AI extracts and organizes your responses into structured fields.

### Chapter Progression

#### Stage 1: Market Context (Chapters 1-2)
**Chat asks:** "What's happening in your market that's making it harder to stand out?"

**You might answer:**
> "Everyone's selling similar outdoor gear. Customers can't tell the difference between brands. They just buy whatever's cheapest on Amazon."

**AI extracts:**
- Market saturation: High
- Competition: Commodity pricing
- Customer confusion: High

#### Stage 2: Avatar Development (Chapter 3)
**Chat asks:** "Describe your ideal customer's daily life and routines..."

**You might answer:**
> "She's 28-35, works in tech, makes good money but limited free time. Weekend hikes are her escape. She researches gear obsessively but values quality over price."

**AI extracts:**
- Age: 28-35
- Occupation: Tech professional
- Income: $75K-120K
- Values: Quality, Research-driven
- Activities: Weekend hiking

#### Stage 3: IDEA Framework (Chapters 4-5)

The chat explores four pillars:

**Insight:** "What hidden truth about your customer have you discovered?"
> "They buy gear as motivation to actually go hiking, not just for the hike itself."

**Distinctive:** "What makes your brand unmistakably different?"
> "We're the only brand that includes trail guides and trip planning with every purchase."

**Empathetic:** "How do you mirror your customer's emotions?"
> "We understand the guilt of expensive gear gathering dust. Our content celebrates small adventures."

**Authentic:** "What's your genuine brand story?"
> "Founded by weekend warriors who kept buying gear but never using it. We built a brand that gets people outside."

### Manual Field Editing

At any point, you can edit the extracted fields:

**Desktop:** Click any field in the left panel
**Mobile:** Swipe up to access field editor

Fields marked with ✏️ are manually edited and won't be overwritten by AI.

## Step 3: Review and Refine (2 minutes)

### Check Your Avatar Profile

The left panel (desktop) or bottom sheet (mobile) shows:

```
Demographics
├── Age: 28-35 ✏️
├── Gender: Female
├── Income: $75K-120K
└── Location: Urban

Psychographics
├── Values: Quality, Adventure, Work-life balance
├── Interests: Hiking, Photography, Wellness
├── Pain Points: Time constraints, Guilt about unused gear
└── Aspirations: More outdoor time, Fitness goals

Behaviors
├── Shopping: Research-heavy, Quality-focused
├── Media: Instagram, Outdoor blogs, Podcasts
├── Purchase Triggers: Peer recommendations, Sales
└── Brand Loyalty: Medium
```

### Make Manual Adjustments

Click/tap any field to edit. Your changes are saved automatically and marked with ✏️.

## Step 4: Generate Strategy Document (1 minute)

### Desktop
1. Click "Generate Document" in the toolbar
2. Select format (PDF or Markdown)
3. Review and download

### Mobile
1. Tap menu (≡) → Generate Document
2. Choose format
3. Share or save

Your document includes:
- Complete avatar profile
- IDEA framework analysis
- Relevant book excerpts
- Marketing recommendations
- Content strategy
- Channel recommendations

## Common Questions

**Q: How detailed should my answers be?**
A: Natural conversation works best. 2-3 sentences per question is usually enough.

**Q: Can I skip chapters?**
A: Yes! Click "Skip" or jump to any chapter from the progress bar.

**Q: What if the AI gets something wrong?**
A: Simply edit the field manually. Your edit takes priority.

**Q: How many avatars should I create?**
A: Start with 3-5 to test different segments. Most successful users maintain 3-7 active avatars.

**Q: Can I import existing personas?**
A: Yes! Upload PDFs or documents, and the AI will extract relevant information.
