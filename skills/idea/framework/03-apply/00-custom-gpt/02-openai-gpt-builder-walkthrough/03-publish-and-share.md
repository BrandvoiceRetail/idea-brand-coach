# SKILL — Save, Publish & Share (Builder Step 6)

## Purpose
Finalizes the GPT and chooses who can use it. The share settings are the practical decision point — private, link-shared, or public in the store — plus the enterprise org-only option.

---

## Step 6: Save & Publish Your Custom GPT
Once satisfied with the setup, save and finalize your custom GPT.

**Steps to Publish & Share Your Custom GPT:**
- Click **Create** to save the GPT settings.
- Select **Share Settings:**
  - **Invite Only** — Restrict access to specific users.
  - **Anyone with the Link** — Share it within a closed group.
  - **Public in GPT Store** — Make it discoverable to all users.
- Click **Update** to finalize changes.
- **Enterprise users** can limit sharing to their organization for internal use only.

## Coaching Application
Match the share setting to the GPT's job: an internal content/brand-strategy assistant stays Invite Only or org-only; a customer-facing helper may go to "Anyone with the Link" or the public store. Decide this deliberately — a brand assistant trained on internal positioning and worksheet data should not be made public by default.
