# SKILL — Test, Modify, Delete & Final Thoughts (Builder Step 7 + maintenance)

## Purpose
Closes the builder walkthrough: the live-testing refinement loop (Step 7), the housekeeping of editing or removing a GPT, and the closing case for why a custom GPT is worth building at all.

---

## Step 7: Test & Refine Your Custom GPT
Once the custom GPT is live, run test conversations to evaluate response quality (as discussed in the strategic track).

**Refinement Tips:**
- **Check for consistency** — Ensure responses align with the brand's tone.
- **Adjust instructions** — Modify guidelines if the AI deviates from expected behavior.
- **Monitor accuracy** — Cross-check factual responses against uploaded knowledge sources.

## How to Modify or Delete a Custom GPT
If you need to edit or delete your GPT, follow these steps:

**Editing a Custom GPT**
- In the side panel, select the GPT you want to modify.
- Click the GPT's name at the top of the chat window.
- Choose **Edit GPT** to make adjustments.
- Click **Update** to save changes.

**Deleting a Custom GPT**
- Select the GPT you want to remove.
- Click **Edit GPT** in the settings menu.
- Choose **Delete GPT**, then confirm.

## Final Thoughts: Why Create a Custom GPT?
> "Building a custom GPT allows users to move beyond standard AI interactions and create a truly tailored experience. Whether for business operations, customer support, or creative content, a personalized AI assistant can enhance efficiency, maintain brand consistency, and improve response accuracy."

By utilizing custom instructions, knowledge uploads, and AI-driven automations, businesses can leverage GPT technology in a way that aligns perfectly with their goals — delivering smart, context-aware responses at scale.

## Coaching Application
The Step 7 refinement loop mirrors the strategic track's Test/Fine-Tune step — same discipline, applied in the live UI: check tone, adjust instructions, cross-check facts against the uploaded worksheet. Keep editing access tight, since the GPT carries the brand's voice and positioning.
