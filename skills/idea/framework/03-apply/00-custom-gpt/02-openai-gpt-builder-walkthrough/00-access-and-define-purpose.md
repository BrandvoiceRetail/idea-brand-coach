# SKILL — Access the GPT Builder & Define Purpose (Builder Steps 1–2)

## Purpose
The starting point of the no-code OpenAI GPT Builder walkthrough — the mechanical UI track that sits alongside the strategic training track. Gets the founder logged in, into the builder, and through the first purpose-and-behavior setup using the Create/Preview split screen.

---

## No-Code Build Intro
> "Custom GPTs provide a more tailored and efficient AI experience compared to standard ChatGPT, offering greater control over responses, branding, and specific use cases."

Users with a ChatGPT Plus or Enterprise account can already tweak responses using custom instructions, but a custom GPT allows for even greater customization. Here's how to build and configure your own custom GPT with advanced settings and knowledge uploads — **no coding required.**

## Step 1: Log in to OpenAI and Access the GPT Builder
- Go to **chat.openai.com** and sign in with your ChatGPT Plus or Enterprise account.
- In the left-hand side panel, select **Explore GPTs**.
- Click **Create** to begin building your AI assistant.

## Step 2: Define the GPT's Purpose & Behavior
Once inside the GPT builder, you'll see a split-screen layout:
- The **Create Panel** is where you input initial instructions.
- The **Preview Panel** allows you to test and refine responses in real time.

**Instructions for Setting Up Your Custom GPT:**
- **Enter a name and description** — Define the chatbot's function and primary role.
- **Provide an initial set of instructions** — Outline how your GPT should respond to different queries.
- **Test interactions in the preview panel** and refine responses as needed.

> Example: If you're building a customer service AI, instruct it to use polite, concise language and direct users to specific knowledge sources.

## Coaching Application
This is the UI counterpart to the strategic track: the "initial set of instructions" in Step 2 is where the founder pastes the system-instructions scaffold. Use the Preview Panel as a live test loop from the very first instruction — don't wait until the end to find out the GPT is off-brand.
