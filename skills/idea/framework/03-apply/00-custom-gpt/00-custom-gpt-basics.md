# SKILL — Custom GPT Basics: Why Build One

## Purpose
Establishes what a custom GPT is and why it beats standard ChatGPT for brand work. This is the "why bother" before the build — the three concrete benefits a founder gets by creating a dedicated BrandGPT instead of re-prompting plain ChatGPT every time.

---

## Custom GPT vs. Standard ChatGPT
> "Custom GPTs provide a more tailored and efficient AI experience compared to standard ChatGPT, offering greater control over responses, branding, and specific use cases."

Users with a ChatGPT Plus or Enterprise account can already tweak responses using **custom instructions**, but a custom GPT allows for even greater customization. A custom GPT is an ideal tool for businesses, content creators, and professionals who need AI-generated responses tailored to their specific needs.

## The Three Benefits

### 1. Create Multiple Custom GPTs
Unlike traditional custom instructions — which apply a single set of rules to all conversations — a custom GPT lets you build **multiple AI assistants for different purposes.** Whether you need an AI for marketing, customer support, or content creation, you can seamlessly switch between different GPTs to get task-specific assistance.

### 2. Upload Knowledge Sources for Smarter Responses
Rather than manually pasting reference materials into every prompt, you can **upload knowledge files** to your custom GPT. This enables AI to pull directly from these sources, ensuring more relevant, fact-based responses without requiring repeated manual input.

### 3. Share Your Custom GPT with Others
Once your custom GPT is fine-tuned to meet your requirements, you can share it with colleagues, clients, or the wider community. Depending on your privacy settings, you can keep it private, share it via an invite-only link, or make it public within the GPT store.

Frame the custom GPT as the founder's reusable brand asset, not a one-off chat. The upload-knowledge benefit is the hook into the rest of this track: the knowledge file you upload is the completed IDEA Brand Canvas Worksheet — see [[01-idea-ai-training-workflow/00-prepare-the-worksheet]].
