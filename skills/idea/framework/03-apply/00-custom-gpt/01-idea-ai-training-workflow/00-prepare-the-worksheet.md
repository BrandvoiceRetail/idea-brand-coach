# SKILL — Prepare the IDEA Brand Canvas Worksheet (Step 1)

## Purpose
The worksheet is the master AI-training document. Before any AI is trained, every brand input must be compiled into one structured file — because the quality of that file is what guarantees on-brand AI output. This skill is the input checklist: exactly what goes into the worksheet before it becomes a BrandGPT knowledge source.

---

## The Worksheet Is the Foundation
> "To ensure AI-generated content aligns perfectly with your brand's strategy, differentiation, and emotional impact, it must be trained using structured inputs. This is where the IDEA Brand Canvas Worksheet becomes your most powerful tool."

The IDEA Brand Canvas Worksheet is the central document where all research, customer insights, and brand guidelines are recorded. It is the foundation for training AI and **the document you will upload to create your custom BrandGPT.**

## The Four AI-Response Guarantees
By feeding AI this structured data, you ensure every AI-generated response remains:

- **Insight-Driven** — Rooted in deep customer motivations and psychological triggers.
- **Distinctive** — Clearly differentiated from competitors.
- **Empathetic** — Emotionally resonant with customer needs and values.
- **Authentic** — True to the brand's vision, mission, and long-term commitments.

## The Input Checklist
Before training AI, all brand guidelines, customer insights, and messaging principles should be compiled into the worksheet across four input areas:

### Brand Positioning & Differentiation
- **Unique Value Proposition (UVP)** — What makes the brand different?
- **Competitive Analysis** — Who are your competitors, and how do you stand apart?
- **Brand Promise** — What do customers expect when engaging with your brand?

### Customer Insights (Avatar 2.0 Tool)
- **Target Audience Profile** — Demographics, psychographics, emotional triggers.
- **Buying Motivations** — Functional and emotional needs.
- **Behavioral Science Insights** — Social proof, loss aversion, trust signals.

### Brand Voice & Messaging Guidelines
- **Tone of Voice** — Inspirational, authoritative, friendly, luxurious, etc.
- **Emotional Resonance** — How should messaging make customers feel?
- **Key Phrases & Brand Language** — Unique terminology or taglines.

### Additional Brand Statements (as described in Chapters 5 and 6)
- Brand Purpose
- Brand Vision
- Brand Mission
- Brand Values
- Brand Positioning
- Value Proposition
- Brand Personality

> "Once this worksheet is complete, it becomes the master strategy document used to train AI."

## Coaching Application
Do not let the founder train AI on a thin or improvised brief. Walk the checklist as a gate: every line should already be answered by their Avatar 2.0 (Chapter 3) and Brand Canvas / Brand Voice work (Chapters 5–6). If a field is empty, the gap will show up as generic AI output later. A complete worksheet here is what makes Steps 2–7 work.
