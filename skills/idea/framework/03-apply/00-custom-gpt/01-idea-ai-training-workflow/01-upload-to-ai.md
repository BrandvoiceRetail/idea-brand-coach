# SKILL — Upload the Worksheet to AI (Step 2)

## Purpose
The three-step upload procedure that turns the completed worksheet into AI training data. This is the mechanical hand-off between "worksheet is ready" and "AI is being instructed" — short, but it sets up the system-instructions step that follows.

---

## The Procedure
Now that you have your IDEA Brand Canvas Worksheet ready, the next step is to upload it into AI for training.

> NOTE: Detailed, step-by-step guidance is provided at the end of this chapter (the OpenAI GPT Builder walkthrough).

1. **Open Your AI Model** (e.g., ChatGPT or a Custom GPT Platform).
2. **Upload the IDEA Brand Canvas Worksheet** as a reference document.
3. **Provide AI with a structured set of System Instructions** to ensure it follows the correct framework.

## Coaching Application
Stress that uploading the worksheet alone is not enough — step 3 (system instructions) is what makes the AI behave like the brand rather than just having access to a document. The next skill provides the full reusable system-instructions scaffold. For the click-by-click upload mechanics, the founder cross-references the GPT Builder walkthrough.
