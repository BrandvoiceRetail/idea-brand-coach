# SKILL — Share Relatable Stories

## Purpose
The second Empathetic step: use personal story to connect emotionally. The asset is the three-element story framework — preserve it verbatim as the structure for every brand story.

---

## Why Stories
> "Stories are one of the most effective ways to connect emotionally. Share your experiences, including the struggles and lessons that shaped you."

## Action Plan for Storytelling
**Exercise:** Write a story about a pivotal moment in your career or life. Frame it around three key elements:

- The challenge you faced.
- The steps you took to overcome it.
- The outcome and how it relates to your audience's journey.

**Outcome:** Empathy helps your audience feel understood, building trust and strengthening their connection to your brand.

## Coaching Application
The third element is the one founders skip — without "how it relates to your audience's journey," a story is just a memoir. Enforce all three beats. Use it as a repeatable template: any pivotal moment (a failure, a turning point, a hard-won lesson) maps onto challenge → steps → outcome-for-them. This pairs directly with the Authentic pillar's vulnerability step, where the challenge is an honest failure.
