# SKILL — Speak Their Language

## Purpose
The first Empathetic step: mirror how your audience actually talks instead of hiding behind jargon. Includes the copy-audit and audience-listening action plan.

---

## Mirror, Don't Lecture
> "Avoid industry jargon or overly technical language unless your audience appreciates it. Instead, mirror how your audience speaks and thinks."

## Action Plan for Relatable Communication
- Review your social media posts and website copy. Are they written in a way your audience would naturally talk? If not, revise.
- Use tools like polls, Q&A sessions, and surveys to find out how your audience feels and what they want to learn.

## Coaching Application
Run this as a literal audit: pull the founder's existing posts and page copy, flag jargon and corporate phrasing, and rewrite in the audience's own words. The polls/Q&A/surveys loop keeps the language current — it's a listening mechanism, not a one-time pass. Feed the captured phrasing back into the signature-style voice and the relatable stories.
