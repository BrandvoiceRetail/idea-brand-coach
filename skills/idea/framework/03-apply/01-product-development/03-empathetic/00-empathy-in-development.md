# SKILL — Empathy in Product Development

## Purpose
The third IDEA pillar applied to product development: empathy as a design lens applied across the whole customer journey, not just the product in isolation.

---

## The Principle
The third element of the IDEA framework, **empathy**, is about understanding your customer's **emotional and practical needs throughout their journey.** When developing a product, empathy ensures that **every feature and interaction is designed with the customer in mind**, helping them overcome pain points by creating a positive emotional experience.

## Coaching Application
Treat empathy as a lens applied across the entire journey — discovery, purchase, and post-purchase — not a single feature bolted on at the end. For each touchpoint, ask the empathetic question: "What does the customer really need here, and where does it hurt?" The four designing-empathetic-products tactics operationalize this lens.
