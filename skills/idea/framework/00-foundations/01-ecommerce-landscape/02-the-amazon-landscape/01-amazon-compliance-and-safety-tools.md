# SKILL — Amazon's Product-Safety & Compliance Tools

## Purpose
A reference list of the concrete programs Amazon uses to police product safety and compliance. Useful for sellers who want to understand the trust/safety machinery they operate inside and report bad actors. Preserve the list verbatim.

---

## The Trusted Expert Network (TEN)
In response to the non-compliant-import crisis, Amazon launched the **Amazon Trusted Expert Network (TEN)** in the EU — an **invitation-only** program empowering industry professionals and sellers to identify and report listings that pose safety or compliance risks. Participants use **Compliance Central** to report problematic products with detailed descriptions, **URLs or ASINs, and supporting documentation such as lab reports.**

## Global Safety & Compliance Initiatives (verbatim)
> - **Manage Your Compliance Dashboard:** a centralized compliance dashboard letting sellers upload and manage product-safety documentation efficiently, ensuring adherence to regulations.
> - **A-to-Z Guarantee Program:** customers covered for defective or unsafe products through Amazon's refund/claims resolution program, including protection against personal injury or property damage.
> - **Product Safety Pledges:** partnerships with governments in Australia, Canada, the EU, and Japan to set higher standards for online marketplaces.
> - **Proactive Monitoring & AI:** machine-learning models and real-time compliance monitoring to detect and prevent unsafe or defective products.
> - **Collaboration with Regulatory Authorities:** working with consumer-protection agencies worldwide to enhance safety standards and streamline recalls.

## Coaching Application
These programs are part of the trust infrastructure of the marketplace. A compliant, well-documented seller benefits from the safety reputation; reporting unsafe competitors via Compliance Central protects both customers and the category.
