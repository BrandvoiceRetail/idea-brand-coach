# SKILL — Mistrust Is the Default State

## Purpose
Sets the macro context for the whole book: today's consumer starts from cynicism, not openness. Use it to explain *why* trust-building (not louder marketing) is the job — the market a seller is entering is hostile to claims by default.

---

## The Landscape
> "In a world where trust in corporations and institutions has seriously eroded, today's consumers crave more than transactional relationships with brands; they want trust, empathy, and meaningful connections."

The rise of consumer skepticism is driven by **scandals, data breaches, fake reviews, and the impersonal nature of AI-driven interactions** — which has made **authenticity the cornerstone of successful branding.**

## Cynicism Is Now the Default
Consumers face a deluge of advertising, fake reviews, and misinformation, so **cynicism has become the default state.** Research shows trust in traditional institutions, including corporations, has plummeted; data-privacy and transparency scandals have deepened the distrust.

## The Examples That Eroded Trust
- **Facebook / Cambridge Analytica** — user data exploited for political purposes; Meta added privacy tools, yet skepticism persisted.
- **Meta ends third-party fact-checking (Jan 2025)** — replaced with "Community Notes" across Facebook, Instagram, Threads; intended to reduce censorship but raised fears of misinformation spreading.
- **Twitter/X & YouTube** — algorithms sometimes prioritize sensational or polarizing content over accurate information.
- **TikTok** — scrutiny over data-sharing with China; national-security/surveillance fears (and possible loss of US operation).
- **Generative AI** — deepfakes and synthetic media that can manipulate public opinion.

These reflect a broader sentiment: that many platforms **prioritize profits and engagement metrics over user safety, transparency, and ethical responsibility.**

## Coaching Application
Frame the seller's starting position honestly: customers assume the worst until proven otherwise. The entire IDEA Framework exists to move a customer from default mistrust to earned trust. Don't fight skepticism with bigger claims — defuse it with authenticity, transparency, and proof.
