# SKILL — The Four Questions Every Customer Is Silently Asking

## Purpose
A diagnostic checklist. Customers run these four questions before they buy. A brand that answers all four "with clarity and confidence" breaks through hesitation. Use it as a pre-launch audit for any listing, page, or campaign.

---

## The Four Questions
Customers today don't just want a product — they want a relationship. They're asking themselves:

1. **Does this brand understand my needs?**
2. **Can I trust this product to deliver what I want?**
3. **Will I regret buying this?**
4. **Do they really "get" me?**

> "If your brand can answer those questions with clarity and confidence, you'll break through hesitation — the silent killer of sales conversion."

## Coaching Application
Treat these as an acceptance test. For any customer-facing asset, point to the specific copy, proof, or design element that answers each question:

- Q1 (understand my needs) → insight-driven messaging, Avatar 2.0 work.
- Q2 (can I trust it to deliver) → proof, reviews, guarantees.
- Q3 (will I regret this) → risk removal — returns, trials, warranties.
- Q4 (do they get me) → empathy, identity, voice that mirrors the customer.

Any question without a clear answer is a conversion leak to fix.
