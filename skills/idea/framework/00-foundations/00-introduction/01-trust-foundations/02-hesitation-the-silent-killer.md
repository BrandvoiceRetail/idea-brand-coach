# SKILL — Hesitation: The Silent Killer of Conversion

## Purpose
Names the real enemy. Lost sales usually aren't lost to a competitor — they're lost to hesitation. This skill keeps the coaching focused on removing doubt, not just adding features.

---

## The Concept
Hesitation is "the silent killer of sales conversion." Unresolved doubt — not a better competitor — is what stalls most purchases. The cure is trust answered clearly enough to break through the hesitation (see [[01-the-four-customer-questions]]).

## The Three Symptoms of a Trust Gap
> "But without trust:
> - Customers abandon their carts.
> - You're stuck competing on price, which isn't sustainable.
> - Your ads work harder, but your results don't improve."

These three symptoms are the observable signature of hesitation. When a seller reports them, the root cause is a trust/hesitation problem, not a traffic problem.

## Coaching Application
Map each symptom to its fix:
- **Cart abandonment** → remove friction and risk at the decision stage.
- **Price competition** → build distinctive, emotional value so price stops being the only axis.
- **Ads working harder for the same result** → fix the trust deficit the ads are pouring traffic into.

> "This is the problem Authentically Human Branding solves... building trust at every stage of the customer journey — from the moment they discover your brand to when they become loyal advocates."
