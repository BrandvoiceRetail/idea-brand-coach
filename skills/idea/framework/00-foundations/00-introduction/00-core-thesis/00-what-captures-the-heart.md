# SKILL — What Captures the Heart Goes in the Cart

## Purpose
The book's central thesis and the single sentence the whole IDEA Framework serves. Use it to anchor every brand decision: people buy on emotion first. If a brand strategy does not move the heart, it will not move the cart.

---

## The Thesis
"What captures the heart goes in the cart" is more than a catchy phrase — it is a fundamental truth about how customers make decisions. Purchasing isn't merely a rational process; it's deeply emotional.

> "We buy from brands that spark something within us — trust, excitement, belonging, relief, security, or even nostalgia. The brands that cultivate these emotional connections don't simply sell more; they create lifelong advocates."

## The Six Emotions Brands Can Spark
A brand earns the cart by sparking one or more of these feelings:

- Trust
- Excitement
- Belonging
- Relief
- Security
- Nostalgia

## Why It Matters
In today's marketplace — where consumers are bombarded with choices and overwhelmed with marketing messages — a great product and competitive pricing are no longer enough. Emotional connection is the differentiator. Brands that cultivate it don't just sell more; they create lifelong advocates.

## Coaching Application
When evaluating any piece of brand work (a listing, an ad, a name, a product), ask: *Which heart-emotion does this spark, and for whom?* If the honest answer is "none — it just states features or price," the work is not yet ready.
