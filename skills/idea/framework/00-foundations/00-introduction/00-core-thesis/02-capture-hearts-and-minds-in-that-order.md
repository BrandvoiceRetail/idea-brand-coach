# SKILL — Capture Hearts AND Minds (In That Order)

## Purpose
The sequencing rule for all messaging. Emotion comes first, reason second — never the reverse. This governs how to order any piece of brand communication.

---

## The Rule
> "Authentically Human brands use the power of emotional triggers to capture the hearts and minds (in that order!) of customers to drive sales conversions and create loyal customers."

The parenthetical "(in that order!)" is the operative instruction: **win the heart, then give the mind permission to agree.**

## Why the Order Is Fixed
Because 95% of decisions are subconscious and emotion-led (see [[01-buying-is-emotional-95-percent-subconscious]]), leading with logic asks the customer to do the hard work the emotion was supposed to do. Lead with the feeling; then supply the rational justification the mind needs to feel safe.

## Coaching Application
When structuring a listing, ad, or landing page:
1. **Heart first** — open with the emotional trigger, transformation, or identity payoff.
2. **Mind second** — follow with the specs, proof, and guarantees that justify the feeling.

A version that opens with a spec sheet has inverted the order and will convert worse.
