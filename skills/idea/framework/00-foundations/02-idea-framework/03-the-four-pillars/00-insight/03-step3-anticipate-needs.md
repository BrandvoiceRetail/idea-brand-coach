# SKILL — Insight Step 3: Anticipate Customer Needs

## Purpose
The third step of the Insight pillar: move from reactive to proactive by tracking shifts in behavior, culture, and technology to introduce solutions before customers even realize they need them. Use it to push a brand from following demand to anticipating it.

---

## Be Proactive, Not Reactive

Brands must **anticipate future customer needs, not just react to current ones.** This requires tracking shifts in consumer behavior, cultural trends, and technological advancements. By being proactive, brands can introduce solutions before customers even realize they need them.

Successful brands don't just respond to current demands; they anticipate future customer needs, staying ahead of consumer expectations. This requires closely monitoring:

- **Shifts in consumer behavior**
- **Cultural trends**
- **Technological advancements**

...so they can introduce solutions before customers even realize they want them.

## Coaching Application
Reactive brands ask "what are customers asking for?" Proactive brands ask "where is behavior, culture, and technology heading, and what will customers need next?" When a brand owner is purely chasing current demand, point them at the three signals to monitor — the anticipation is where category leadership comes from (see the worked examples skill).
