# SKILL — Personalized, Empathetic Customer Experiences

## Purpose
The fourth of four trust strategies. Treat customers as individuals, not numbers in a sales funnel — personalization signals that you see and understand them. Preserve the Netflix example.

---

## The Strategy
Customers who feel seen and understood are more likely to trust a brand. Personalization shows that you care about your customers **as individuals, not just as numbers in a sales funnel.** It can take many forms, from **tailored product recommendations to customized marketing messages** based on customer preferences and behaviors.

## Example — Netflix
> "Netflix is a master of personalization. Its algorithm-driven recommendations are designed to make each user feel like Netflix understands their unique tastes. This personalization creates a more engaging, customer-centric experience, increasing loyalty and trust in the platform."

## Coaching Application
Personalization is the operational proof of empathy: it converts "we understand you" from a claim into an experience. Start with the highest-signal moment — recommendations or messaging keyed to actual behavior — so each customer feels individually seen. The goal is the feeling of being understood, not the volume of data collected.
