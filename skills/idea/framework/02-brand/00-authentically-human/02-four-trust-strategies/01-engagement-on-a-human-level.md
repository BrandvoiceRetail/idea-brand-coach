# SKILL — Engagement on a Human Level

## Purpose
The second of four trust strategies. Show the real people behind and using your products so the brand feels approachable, not faceless. Preserve the Glossier example.

---

## The Strategy
Humanize your brand by showing that **real people are behind the products and services.** Let customers see the faces behind the company — whether through **employee stories, behind-the-scenes content, or highlighting the real people who use your products.** Personal engagement helps break down barriers and makes your brand feel more approachable.

## Example — Glossier
> "Glossier, the beauty brand, excels at this by encouraging community involvement and featuring user-generated content. By showcasing real people using their products and engaging directly with customers on social media, Glossier builds a sense of connection and community."

## Coaching Application
Find the faces the brand is currently hiding: founders, employees, and real customers. Employee stories and behind-the-scenes content humanize the company side; user-generated content humanizes the customer side. Direct, two-way engagement on social — not broadcast — is what turns audience into community.
