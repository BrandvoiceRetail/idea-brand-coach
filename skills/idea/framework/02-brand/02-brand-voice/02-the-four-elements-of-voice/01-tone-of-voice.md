# SKILL — Tone of Voice (The Emotional Signature)

## Purpose
Defines tone as the brand's emotional signature and illustrates it with three tone exemplars and the TAG Heuer case study. Use this to help a seller choose and articulate a consistent emotional register.

---

## Tone Is the Emotional Signature
> "Tone of voice reflects how a brand speaks to its audience, shaping its personality and emotional resonance. Whether authoritative and sophisticated (like Rolex), energetic and dynamic (Red Bull), or friendly and playful (Innocent Drinks), tone sets the expectations for how customers engage with the brand."

Three tone exemplars:

| Brand | Tone |
|---|---|
| **Rolex** | authoritative and sophisticated |
| **Red Bull** | energetic and dynamic |
| **Innocent Drinks** | friendly and playful |

## Case Study: TAG Heuer (Tone)
> "TAG Heuer luxury sports watches is a brand that fuses precision, performance, and prestige. The brand's tone is bold, assertive, and infused with the energy of sport, aligning with its association with elite athleticism and competitive excellence. Through partnerships with world-class athletes and events, including Formula 1's Red Bull Racing Team, the TAG Heuer reinforces its identity as one built for those who thrive under pressure. Its campaigns exude confidence, technical mastery, and resilience, ensuring TAG Heuer is not just a watchmaker—it is a symbol of ambition and success."

Key tone signals from the case study:
- **Fusion:** precision, performance, and prestige.
- **Tone:** bold, assertive, infused with the energy of sport.
- **Proof:** partnerships with world-class athletes and events, including **Formula 1's Red Bull Racing Team.**
- **Result:** "not just a watchmaker—it is a symbol of ambition and success."

## Coaching Application
Ask the seller to name their brand's tone in two or three adjectives, then point to a tone exemplar they admire (Rolex, Red Bull, or Innocent set the spectrum from sophisticated to playful). Use TAG Heuer as the model: the tone (bold, assertive, sport-energized) is backed by real proof (elite athlete and F1 Red Bull Racing partnerships) so the emotional signature feels earned, not claimed.
