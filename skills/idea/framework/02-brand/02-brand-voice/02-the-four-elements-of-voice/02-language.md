# SKILL — Language (The Brand's Distinctive Expression)

## Purpose
Shows how language — taglines and copy — reinforces brand identity and turns a functional product into an emotional experience. Use this to help a seller write aspirational, identity-reinforcing copy.

---

## Language Reinforces Identity
> "Language is the foundation of brand storytelling. Every tagline, product description, and customer interaction should reinforce the brand's identity. Luxury brands, for instance, use aspirational language to evoke exclusivity and sophistication."

## The TAG Heuer Tagline
> "TAG Heuer's famous tagline, 'Don't Crack Under Pressure,' transforms a functional watch into an emotional experience—a symbol of resilience and determination. By using active, aspirational language and celebrity endorsements, TAG Heuer aligns its messaging with customers who strive for excellence and high performance."

The tagline **"Don't Crack Under Pressure"** does the work: it converts a watch's function into resilience and determination, and speaks to customers who strive for excellence and high performance.

## Coaching Application
Push the seller past feature-listing. Every tagline and product description should reinforce identity and, like "Don't Crack Under Pressure," translate a functional benefit into an emotional one (resilience, determination, belonging). Favor active, aspirational language; check that it speaks to who the customer wants to become, not just what the product does.
