# SKILL — Brand Personality

## Purpose
Defines the human-like traits that influence how the brand speaks, interacts, and resonates with its audience. Captures the definition, the four Key IDEA Principles, the AI prompt, and the worked sample output — all verbatim.

---

## Definition
A brand personality defines the **human-like traits that influence how the brand speaks, interacts, and resonates with its audience.**

## Key IDEA Principles
- **Insight-Driven:** Based on what attracts and engages the target audience.
- **Distinctive:** Establishes a unique and recognizable brand identity.
- **Empathetic:** Creates an emotional connection through relatable traits.
- **Authentic:** Ensures consistency across all brand communications.

## AI Prompt (Example)
> "Define the brand's personality traits that influence how it interacts with and resonates with its audience. Ensure these traits align with customer expectations and create a distinctive, relatable identity. Use the IDEA framework to make them engaging, emotionally compelling, and consistently reflected across brand touchpoints."

## Sample Output
> "Warm, innovative, approachable, and bold. Our brand is the friendly guide helping customers transition to a sustainable lifestyle, one simple choice at a time."

## Coaching Application
Personality is the input to voice — it answers "if the brand were a person, what are they like?" The sample names four traits ("Warm, innovative, approachable, and bold") and then a role/archetype ("the friendly guide"). Coach the user to do both: a list of adjectives alone is hard to act on, but pairing it with an archetype ("the friendly guide helping customers transition… one simple choice at a time") makes the personality usable. These traits feed directly into Brand Voice.
