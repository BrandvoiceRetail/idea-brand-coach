# SKILL — The IDEA Brand Canvas: Overview & Instructions

## Purpose
Frames the whole chapter: the IDEA Brand Canvas is a fill-in-the-box worksheet that distills emotional triggers, Avatar 2.0, and the IDEA Framework into a single, systematic brand-strategy document. Use this as the entry point — it sets the worksheet rationale, the access link, the two-step instructions, and the critical sequencing rule (Avatar 2.0 + IDEA BEFORE Brand Statements).

---

## Why the Worksheet Exists
> "Let's take all of this and distill it into a simple, fill-in-the boxes worksheet. This is the exact process that I follow with my private clients and Titan Network members, so I know it works well!"

Building a strong, conversion-driven brand strategy doesn't have to be overwhelming — but many sellers struggle with **unclear messaging, weak positioning, and fragmented branding, leading to low trust, poor engagement, and inconsistent sales.**

That's why the IDEA Brand Canvas Worksheet exists: a structured, fill-in-the-box approach that **simplifies and systematizes brand strategy.** Instead of getting lost in complex branding exercises, the worksheet guides you step by step to create a clear, compelling brand identity that resonates with customers and drives conversions. It covers all the critical elements of brand strategy, making it easy to define and refine your brand with clarity and confidence.

## Access the Editable Sheet
> "Here is a quick look but you can access the editable sheet using this link. Remember, you need to make a copy before you can use it!"

Editable worksheet: **https://bit.ly/4ldQSWb**

## Instructions
| Step | What to do |
|------|------------|
| **Step 1** | Work through the IDEA Customer Avatar 2.0 and IDEA Framework and capture your outputs in the boxes/columns below. |
| **Step 2** | Write your brand statements using the AI of your choice. |

## Sequencing Rule (do this in order)
> **NOTE:** "In order to educate AIs on your brand using the IDEA Framework, you need to go through the Avatar 2.0 and IDEA processes described in Chapter 3 and 4 and fill in those sections of the worksheet BEFORE you proceed to the Brand Statements described below so that you have the foundations for your brand established and ready to go."

The order is not optional: **Avatar 2.0 → IDEA Framework canvas → Brand Statements.** The first two tables are the inputs the AI uses to generate the statements; skipping them produces generic, ungrounded output.

## Coaching Application
Confirm the user has made their own copy of the editable sheet before they start typing. Then hold them to the sequence: do not let them jump to writing a brand purpose or mission until the Avatar 2.0 and IDEA Framework boxes are filled — those outputs are the raw material every statement is built from.
