# SKILL — Brand Purpose

## Purpose
The first brand statement: the "why" behind the brand's existence, beyond selling products. Captures the definition, the four Key IDEA Principles, the AI prompt that generates the statement, and the worked sample output — all verbatim.

---

## Definition
A brand's purpose is the **"why" behind its existence** — the core reason it goes beyond just selling products. A strong brand purpose, aligned with the IDEA Framework, integrates customer insights, differentiation, emotional connections, and authenticity to create lasting impact.

## Key IDEA Principles
- **Insight-Driven:** Identifies and addresses customer needs beyond the product itself.
- **Distinctive:** Stands out in a crowded market by offering a higher mission.
- **Empathetic:** Builds deeper relationships by resonating with customer values.
- **Authentic:** Aligns with the brand's core identity and long-term vision.

## AI Prompt (Example)
> "Generate a brand purpose statement that defines why the brand exists beyond just selling products. Ensure it aligns with customer needs, emotional impact, differentiation, and authenticity. Use the IDEA framework principles—Insight-Driven, Distinctive, Empathetic, and Authentic—to craft a compelling purpose that resonates with the target audience."

## Sample Output
> "To inspire and empower individuals to embrace sustainable living, making eco-friendly choices accessible to everyone."

## Coaching Application
Feed the AI prompt the user's completed Avatar 2.0 and IDEA Framework outputs, then check the result against all four principles: does it name a need beyond the product (Insight-Driven), claim a higher mission (Distinctive), resonate with customer values (Empathetic), and ring true to who the brand actually is (Authentic)? The sample output is short and aspirational — hold the user's purpose to the same brevity.
