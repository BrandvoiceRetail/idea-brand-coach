# SKILL — Brand Vision

## Purpose
The second brand statement: an aspirational statement about the future impact the brand seeks to make. Captures the definition, the four Key IDEA Principles, the AI prompt, and the worked sample output — all verbatim.

---

## Definition
A brand vision is an **aspirational statement about the future impact the brand seeks to make.** It reflects the long-term goals that shape decision-making and growth strategies.

## Key IDEA Principles
- **Insight-Driven:** Rooted in a deep understanding of evolving customer expectations.
- **Distinctive:** Sets a unique direction for brand leadership in its category.
- **Empathetic:** Addresses emotional and societal needs to foster a loyal community.
- **Authentic:** Aligns with the brand's long-term commitments and values.

## AI Prompt (Example)
> "Develop an aspirational brand vision statement that defines the long-term impact and future direction of the brand. Ensure it aligns with market trends, customer expectations, and the brand's commitment to innovation, sustainability, or social responsibility. Incorporate the IDEA framework to make it distinctive, emotionally compelling, and strategically insightful."

## Sample Output
> "To become the global leader in sustainable products, transforming the way people shop and live for a healthier planet."

## Coaching Application
Vision is future-facing where purpose is present-facing — keep them distinct when coaching. Press the user to make the vision genuinely aspirational ("become the global leader… transforming the way people shop and live") rather than a restatement of the purpose. Check it sets a unique direction (Distinctive) and stays anchored to long-term commitments the brand can actually honor (Authentic).
