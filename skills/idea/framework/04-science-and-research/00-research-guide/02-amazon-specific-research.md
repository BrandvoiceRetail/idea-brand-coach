# SKILL — Amazon-Specific Research (4 Sources)

## Purpose
The four Amazon data sources that reveal customer expectations, hesitations, demand trends, and search intent. Use this for any Amazon seller or DTC brand researching positioning on-platform.

---

## 1. Amazon Customer Reviews
> "Reviews provide unfiltered insights into customer expectations, disappointments, and product experiences."

**How It Helps**
- Reveals customer frustrations (e.g., poor packaging, unclear instructions)
- Identifies strong emotional connections (e.g., joy, relief, frustration)

**Use in IDEA Framework:** Insight-Driven and Empathetic – Align brand messaging to reinforce positive emotional experiences.
**Tool:** "Use AI tools like **ReviewMeta** to analyze review authenticity and detect patterns across competitors."

## 2. Amazon Q&A Section
> "Shows pre-purchase hesitations and customer concerns before they buy."

**How It Helps:** Identifies areas where customers need clarity or reassurance before making a purchase.
**Use in IDEA Framework:** Empathetic and Insight-Driven – Use this to anticipate objections in your product copy.
**Tool:** "Use AI-driven **'People Also Ask'** tools to generate answers based on the most common customer concerns."

## 3. Amazon Best Seller Rank (BSR) & Competitor Tracking
> "Shows which products resonate emotionally with customers based on sales trends."

**How It Helps**
- Identifies seasonality and emotional triggers (e.g., impulse buys, gifting trends)
- Detects customer desires and trust (best-selling products indicate brand loyalty and perceived value)

**Use in IDEA Framework:** Distinctive – Identify what's trending and position your brand to stand out.
**Tool:** "Use **Helium 10's Black Box** feature to track BSR trends over time."

## 4. Amazon Search Term Reports (For Brand-Registered Sellers)
> "Uncovers how customers search for solutions, revealing intent and motivation."

**How It Helps**
- Detects functional vs. emotional-driven keywords
- Identifies gaps where competitors aren't meeting specific customer needs

**Use in IDEA Framework:** Insight-Driven – Optimize product descriptions and ad copy with emotional triggers that match search intent.
**Tool:** "Use **ChatGPT** to create keyword clusters based on emotional vs. logical search behavior."

## Coaching Application
- Treat the **functional vs. emotional keyword split** as the strategic payoff: functional terms win the click, emotional terms win the heart. Map both.
- Q&A and reviews are pre- and post-purchase mirrors — objections live in Q&A, lived experience lives in reviews.
