# SKILL — Social Listening & UGC

## Purpose
Research beyond the storefront: social conversations capture authentic, real-time emotional reactions in the customer's own language. Use this to harvest the exact phrasing your audience uses about the problem you solve.

---

## Why It's Valuable
> "Social media conversations reflect authentic, real-time emotional reactions to brands and products."

## Where to Find It
- Twitter, Instagram, TikTok, Reddit, Facebook Groups
- Influencer and customer video reviews

## How to Use It
- Identify how people describe products in their own words
- Spot emerging emotional trends (e.g., "I love how this makes me feel")
- Detect customer frustrations competitors may have overlooked

## How to Apply This (IDEA Framework Use Case)
> "**Authentic and Empathetic** – Align marketing with the natural language and emotions used by your audience."

## Tools
> "Use **Sprout Social** or **Brandwatch** for automated sentiment analysis."

## Coaching Application
- The win here is **customer-own-words**: lift verbatim emotional phrasing ("I love how this makes me feel") straight into brand copy — it reads as authentic because it is.
- Watch for frustrations competitors overlooked; that gap is a positioning opening.
