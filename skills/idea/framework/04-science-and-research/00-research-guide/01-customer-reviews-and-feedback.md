# SKILL — Customer Reviews & Feedback

## Purpose
The first and most direct research method: reviews surface the raw emotional language customers use. Use this to mine real pain points, desires, and frustrations instead of guessing them.

---

## Why It's Valuable
> "Customer reviews reveal direct emotional responses—pain points, desires, and frustrations that traditional research often misses."

## Where to Find It
- Amazon, Google, Yelp, Facebook, Reddit, TikTok
- Brand-owned surveys and feedback forms
- Competitor product reviews

## What to Extract
- Identify recurring customer pain points and emotional language
- Understand what customers truly value versus what brands assume
- Pinpoint unmet needs for product or messaging refinement

## How to Apply This (IDEA Framework Use Case)
> "**Insight-Driven and Empathetic** – Identify the exact emotions and motivations that drive customer satisfaction or frustration, then align your messaging to emphasize solutions."

## Tools
> "Use AI-powered sentiment analysis tools like **MonkeyLearn** or **Lexalytics** to analyze large volumes of customer reviews quickly."

## Coaching Application
- Have the seller pull recurring phrases from reviews and feed the customer's *own words* back into product copy.
- The gap between "what customers value" and "what brands assume" is the most actionable signal here — name it explicitly.
