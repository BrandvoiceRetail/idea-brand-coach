# SKILL — Field 4: Shopper Types

## Purpose
The fourth Avatar 2.0 field. Four behavioral archetypes from Shopify's 2024 research let you tailor the customer journey by what each type actually values — beyond surface demographics. Use it to choose the right messaging, pricing, and channel per segment.

---

## Source
> "Shopify's 2024 State of Commerce Report: In 2024 Shopify surveyed 7,628 consumers aged 18 and over. The outcome was a significant step forward in our understanding of the makeup of the online shopping audience in Europe. Whilst the USA was not included in this report, we have used Shopify's four customer typologies to aid us in our understanding."

## The Four Shopper Types (verbatim)
> - **Cost Sensitive Consumers:** Represent **50%** of all consumers, prioritizing price and value above all else. They are not brand loyal.
> - **Quality Focused Consumers:** Comprise **39%**, emphasizing premium products and durability.
> - **Conscious Consumers:** Make up **9%**, valuing ethical practices and sustainability.
> - **Connected Consumers:** Currently the smallest group, accounting for **2%**, engaging with brands digitally and through social communities.

> "These categories illustrate the diverse priorities of modern shoppers and highlight key areas for brands to focus their strategies. Each type is influenced by their generational and socio-demographic profile, shaping their unique motivations and emotional triggers."

## Tailor the Journey by Type
> "These behavioral archetypes provide actionable insights that go beyond surface-level demographics. For instance, understanding that a 'Conscious Consumer' prioritizes sustainability helps brands tailor messaging and product offerings to highlight eco-friendly practices."

> "By integrating these shopper type insights, Avatar 2.0 allows brands to craft specific journeys. For example, cost-sensitive consumers might respond to dynamic pricing strategies or discounts, while connected consumers engage more deeply with social media storytelling and interactive brand content."

## Coaching Application
Identify which of the four types dominates your audience, then match the journey to it: discounts and dynamic pricing for Cost Sensitive, durability and premium proof for Quality Focused, ethical and sustainability claims for Conscious, social storytelling and interactive content for Connected.
