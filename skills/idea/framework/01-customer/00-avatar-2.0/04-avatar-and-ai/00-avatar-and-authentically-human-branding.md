# SKILL — Avatar 2.0 and Authentically Human Branding

## Purpose
Connects the Avatar 2.0 tool to the book's north-star approach: aligning intent, motivation, and triggers is what makes branding feel authentically human. Use this to frame the avatar as the engine of empathy in a culture of distrust.

---

## Avatar 2.0 Powers Empathy
> "Avatar 2.0 works in tandem with the IDEA Framework to craft branding that feels authentically human, fostering trust and emotional resonance. By aligning insights on intent, motivation, and emotional triggers, brands can communicate with customers in a way that reflects genuine understanding and empathy."

## Why It Matters in a Culture of Distrust
> "In today's landscape, where trust in corporations and institutions is at an all-time low, authenticity has become a non-negotiable element of branding. Gerald Zaltman, in his book *How Customers Think*, emphasizes that 95% of consumer decisions occur in the subconscious, making emotional resonance vital."

> "Customers demand transparency, shared values, and meaningful connections—qualities that are often missing from traditional marketing approaches."

## Coaching Application
Use the avatar's three core fields together — intent, motivation, triggers — to communicate genuine understanding. In a low-trust market where 95% of decisions are subconscious, authenticity isn't optional: deliver transparency, shared values, and real connection, or be ignored.
