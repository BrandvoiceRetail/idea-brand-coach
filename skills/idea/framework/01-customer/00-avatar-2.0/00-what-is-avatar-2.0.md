# SKILL — What Is Avatar 2.0

## Purpose
Defines the customer-understanding tool that the entire IDEA Framework starts from. Avatar 2.0 replaces demographic stereotyping with a forensic, behavior-driven customer profile. Use this as the entry point before working any of the five fields.

---

## The Role of Avatar 2.0
> "We should always start with the customer. To that end I have created a new tool for better understanding our customer and what they want. It's called Avatar 2.0 and is designed to integrate with the IDEA Framework."

Avatar 2.0 feeds the IDEA Framework — its job is to **gather and record all relevant data about the customer**.

## The Five Recorded Fields (the diagram)
Avatar 2.0 records the customer across five fields:

| # | Field | Captures |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Buyer Intent** | Search — the "why" behind the query |
| 2 | **Motivations** | Tasks to be achieved by the product |
| 3 | **Emotional Triggers** | The feelings that drive the decision |
| 4 | **Shopper Type 1–4** | Behavioral segment |
| 5 | **Relevant Demographics** | Context, not stereotypes |

## From Guessing to Knowing
> "The IDEA Framework's adoption of Avatar 2.0 marks a shift from outdated demographic segmentation to a forensic, behavior-driven understanding of customers."

Instead of superficial traits like age, income, or marital status, Avatar 2.0 builds a profile rooted in **customer intent, emotional triggers, and motivations derived from their interactions, behaviors, and subconscious cues.**

> "This section explains how the five core aspects—buyer intent, buyer motivation, emotional triggers, shopper types, and relevant demographics—combine to create a powerful toolkit for precision marketing."

## Coaching Application
Always start with the customer. Before writing any positioning or copy, fill all five Avatar 2.0 fields from observed behavior — not assumptions. The shift this tool demands is from *guessing* who the customer is to *knowing*, forensically, what drives them.
