# SKILL — The Subconscious Search for Comfort

## Purpose
Names the underlying drive beneath all the neurochemistry: shoppers are subconsciously seeking to feel good, safe, and connected. Use this as the single sentence that explains *what* the customer is really shopping for.

---

## The Core Drive
> "At the core of these processes is the subconscious drive to feel good, safe, and connected. Shoppers aren't just looking for products; they're seeking emotional highs, comfort, and reassurance that align with their deeper desires."

## Why It Matters
> "By understanding how biochemistry influences behavior, brands can craft messaging, experiences, and products that not only meet customer needs but also resonate on a chemical and emotional level, fostering loyalty and driving long-term success."

## Coaching Application
Behind every search is a person trying to feel good, safe, and connected. Design the whole experience — message, product, and post-purchase — to deliver those three feelings. Meet the functional need *and* the emotional one, and loyalty follows.
