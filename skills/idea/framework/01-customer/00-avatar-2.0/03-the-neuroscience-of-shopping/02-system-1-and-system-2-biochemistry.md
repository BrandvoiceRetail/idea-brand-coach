# SKILL — System 1, System 2, and Biochemistry

## Purpose
Explains why emotional appeals work fast: neurochemical responses bypass slow, logical "System 2" thinking and fire automatic "System 1" processes. Use this to justify leading with emotion — decisions form before logic engages.

---

## Emotion Bypasses Logic (Kahneman)
> "These neurochemical responses often bypass our logical, deliberate 'System 2' thinking (as described by Daniel Kahneman) and activate our automatic, emotional 'System 1' processes. This is why emotional appeals in marketing can have such an immediate impact, driving decisions before we even consciously process them."

## Three Trigger → Chemical Examples (verbatim)
> - An ad featuring a happy family sharing a meal (**belonging**) creates an emotional connection, activating **oxytocin** and making the product feel trustworthy.
> - A limited-time offer (**anticipation**) sparks **dopamine**, motivating immediate action to capture the reward.
> - A guarantee of satisfaction (**relief**) reduces **cortisol**, making the choice feel risk-free.

## Coaching Application
Lead with the emotional cue, because System 1 fires before System 2 reasons. Use the three proven pairings as templates: show belonging to build oxytocin trust, add anticipation/scarcity to spark dopamine action, and offer a guarantee to drop cortisol and make the choice feel safe.
