# SKILL — Context and Timing

## Purpose
The principle that decisions are context-dependent: a product's value is not just its features but how well it fits the customer's life *at that moment*. Brands that read the context can tailor message, design, and timing so the switch feels natural and necessary. "Sensitive timing is everything!"

---

## The Principle
> "Customers don't make decisions in a vacuum—they evaluate products based on their current situation, needs, and timing. A product's value isn't just about its features but how well it fits into a customer's life at that moment."

> "Brands that understand the context of their customers' decisions can position their products more effectively. This means tailoring messaging, design, and timing to align with where the customer is in their journey, making the transition to a new solution feel natural and necessary. Sensitive timing is everything!"

## Coaching Application
Before launching a message, ask: "What's happening in the customer's life that makes *now* the right moment?" Tax season, the new year, back-to-school, a busy week — context turns a generic offer into a timely necessity. Align message, design, and timing to that moment, not just to the product's features.
