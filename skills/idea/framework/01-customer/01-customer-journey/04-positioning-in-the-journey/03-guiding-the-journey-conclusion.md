# SKILL — Guiding the Journey: The IDEA Lens

## Purpose
The chapter's closing synthesis: viewed through the IDEA Strategic Framework, the customer journey is functional, emotionally resonant, *and* contextually relevant. Brands that recognize and address the emotional tension in decision-making create lasting value in customers' minds.

---

## The Conclusion
> "By understanding the customer journey through the lens of the IDEA Strategic Framework brands can develop products that are not only functional but also emotionally resonant and contextually relevant. Decision-making can be filled with emotional tension, brands that recognize and address this will create lasting value in the minds of their customers."

## Coaching Application
Use IDEA as a three-part check on any journey design: is it **functional** (solves the task), **emotionally resonant** (fulfills the Signpost feeling), and **contextually relevant** (fits the customer's moment)? A journey strong on all three resolves the emotional tension of deciding — and that resolution is where lasting value is created.
