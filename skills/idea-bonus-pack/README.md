# IDEA Bonus Pack — Beyond the Book

A **purchasable add-on** of IDEA brand-coaching skills that go **beyond** Trevor Bradford's book *What Captures The Heart Goes In The Cart*. These are intentionally **separate from the launch library** (`skills/idea/`), which is strictly book-only.

- **Launch product** = `skills/idea/` — 158 atomic skills, every one traceable to a passage in the book.
- **This pack** = 7 skills whose content has **no basis in the book** (verified by the launch library's completeness audit in `skills/idea/_coverage.md`). They are coaching value-adds drawn from elsewhere — sold/loaded separately, not part of the book-only launch.

The launch coach (which consumes `skills/idea/framework/`) does **not** load this folder. Wire these in only for customers who have the bonus pack.

## What's inside & why it's "beyond the book"

| Skill | Why it's a bonus (not in the book) |
|---|---|
| `11-brand-essence-dna.md` | The four-circle Brand Essence / Brand DNA model (Attributes · Benefits · Values · Personality → Essence) is **not in the book** — the IDEA Brand Canvas has no Essence/DNA element. |
| `12-agentic-commerce.md` | The book's Ch1 Rufus/Cosmo material is already covered in the launch library; this skill **extends past the 2025 book** into 2026 agentic commerce (ChatGPT agents, Google Gemini, Walmart Sparky, AI visibility). |
| `behavioral-science-deep-dives/14-kahneman-system1-2.md` | Book-cited core (System 1/2) lives in the launch library; this is a **deeper Kahneman treatment** than the book provides. |
| `behavioral-science-deep-dives/15-cialdini-influence.md` | Book cites Cialdini's reciprocity/social-proof/authority/scarcity in passing; this is the **full Influence framework**, richer than the book. |
| `behavioral-science-deep-dives/17-sutherland-psychologic.md` | Sutherland's *Alchemy* appears **only in the book's Further Reading** (no in-text teaching); this is a standalone deep dive. |
| `behavioral-science-deep-dives/18-lindstrom-sensory.md` | Book cites Lindstrom's "emotion beats logic"; this adds **sensory-branding** depth the book doesn't cover. |
| `behavioral-science-deep-dives/19-cialdini-presuasion.md` | The book cites only Cialdini's *Influence*; **Pre-Suasion is absent** from the book entirely. |

## Format note
These are in the **legacy seed-skill format** (`# SKILL NN — Title` / `## Purpose`), not the atomic frontmatter convention used by the launch library. If this pack is productized, a follow-up pass can decompose and reformat them to match the launch library's standard (YAML frontmatter, one-concept-per-file, `[[cross-links]]`). Kept as-is for now to preserve their content unchanged.

The 12 that traced cleanly to the book were superseded and removed when the book-only library was built; these 7 (no book basis) were retained and moved here as the bonus pack per the launch decision (book-only first, beyond-the-book as a paid add-on).
