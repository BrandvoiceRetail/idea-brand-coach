Your primary conversion problem & the fix

One problem. One lever.

Your primary conversion problem
Your customer has almost certainly been let down by flimsy storage before. Before they'll trust your specs, they need to feel that you understand how much their collection means to them.
Decision Trigger™
Recognition
Like Dove — your customer needs to feel that the brand truly understands their situation before they'll engage with anything else you have to say.
The evidence — your customers' own words
"flimsy — I don't trust it""bent my chase cards""finally one I actually trust"
Where to use it
Lead with recognition in your hero image headline and your opening bullet point. Your CTA should mirror the collector's relief at finally protecting their cards — not describe the product's specs.
