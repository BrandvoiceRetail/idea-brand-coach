## What you actually get

### 1. A free read on where your brand leaks trust
Answer six questions and get an AI interpretation of your brand across the IDEA framework. You'll see, in plain language, where prospects hesitate and why. No account, no card, about two minutes. It's the same diagnostic that grounds everything the coach does next. **Free, for everyone.**

### 2. A coach that knows *your* brand — not a generic chatbot
Your diagnostic, avatars, and uploaded context feed a private knowledge base. The Brand Coach answers from *that* — so its advice is about your brand, your customer, and your trust gaps, not boilerplate. It remembers across sessions and can run brand tools for you on request. Ask "what's my biggest trust gap and how do I fix it?" and get an answer you can act on today. **Starter and up.**

### 3. Turn conversations into deliverables
Pro is where the coach stops talking and starts producing. Reveal your brand **Signature**, build your **Brand Canvas**, generate a senior-strategist-tone **Strategy Document**, run a **Marketing Audit**, and draft on-brand copy and concepts — then export it all to PDF, Markdown, or the Gold Workbook. These are the artifacts you'd normally pay a consultant for. **Pro and up.**

### 4. See how rivals win each touchpoint — and defend your brand
Studio extends the coach across your whole funnel: map every brand asset, score each against the IDEA framework, and run **per-touchpoint competitor agents** that show how rivals handle the same moment and where you can win — grounded in real fetched evidence, not guesses. Then close the gap with a drafted countermeasure and measure the lift. **Studio — founding early-access.** Built and in private testing; founding members get in early at a locked price while we finish hardening it. `[STATUS: not-yet-live for GA]`

## FAQ

**What do I get for free?**
The full Brand Diagnostic and its AI interpretation — no account or card required — plus one diagnostic PDF and a 3-message trial of the Brand Coach so you can feel how it works. When you're ready for ongoing coaching, you create an account and pick a plan.

**Can I upgrade or downgrade later?**
Yes. Move between Starter, Pro, and Studio whenever you want — your brand, avatars, and history carry over. Upgrades unlock immediately; downgrades take effect at your next billing cycle.

**Why are there message and generation limits?**
Each coach message and each generated artifact runs real AI work that costs us money. Quotas (e.g. 50/200 coach messages, 2 strategy docs, 20 copy generations per month) let us keep prices low and the service fast, instead of charging everyone for a few heavy users. They reset monthly, and you'll see your usage in-app. Need more? That's what the next tier is for.

**Who can see my brand data?**
Your diagnostic, avatars, documents, and conversations are private to your account and used only to ground *your* coaching. We don't sell your data or train public models on it. You can export everything (PDF / Markdown / Workbook) and request deletion at any time.

**Can I cancel anytime?**
Yes — cancel in a click, no contract, no cancellation fee. You keep access through the end of the period you've paid for, and your exports are yours to keep.
